Reporter Experience Overview

Relancy provides a streamlined, mobile‑friendly reporting experience designed to reduce friction and encourage timely reporting. Reporters can submit incidents with or without identifying information, depending on policy.

Reporting features

  • Simple, guided intake flow
  • Clear explanations of confidentiality and next steps
  • Attachment support for photos, videos, and documents
  • Optional contact information
  • Anonymous reporting when enabled

Anonymous reporting

When enabled, reporters may submit incidents without providing identifying information. Anonymous reports follow the same routing and severity rules but may have limited follow‑up options.

Policy alignment

Administrators control whether anonymous reporting is allowed and define any restrictions or disclaimers.

Audit logging

Anonymous reports are logged without identifying metadata. All reviewer actions remain fully auditable.
