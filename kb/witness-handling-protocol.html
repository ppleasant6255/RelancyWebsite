Protocol Overview

Witness Handling Protocol ensures that witnesses are interviewed safely, confidentially, and without undue influence. Witness statements are treated as sensitive evidence.

Protocol components

  • Confidential scheduling
  • Neutral interviewer assignment
  • Statement capture and verification
  • Evidence attachment and classification
  • Restricted visibility controls

Operational safeguards

Witness identities are protected from involved parties. Notes and statements cannot be altered after submission.

Audit integrity

All witness interactions are logged with timestamp, actor, and confidentiality level for compliance review.
