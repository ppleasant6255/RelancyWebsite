Transfer Overview

Location transfers affect readiness requirements, risk modeling, routing rules, and visibility boundaries. Relancy ensures that transfers are processed consistently and safely.

Transfer tasks

  • Update location assignment
  • Recalculate readiness score
  • Assign location‑specific training
  • Update visibility and routing rules
  • Notify managers and compliance roles

Operational impact

Location transfers ensure that employees meet local requirements and that dashboards reflect accurate data.

Audit integrity

All transfer events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification for defensibility.
