Manager Dashboards

How Relancy provides managers with visibility into readiness, training, and operational activity.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Manager dashboards give supervisors a clear view of their team’s readiness, training status, and involvement in cases or incidents. These dashboards support proactive leadership and operational accountability.

Dashboard components

  • Team readiness scores
  • Training completion and overdue items
  • Open cases involving direct reports
  • Incident trends by team or location
  • Corrective action assignments

Role-based visibility

Managers see only data related to their direct reports or assigned departments. Sensitive cases may remain restricted depending on policy.

Operational benefits

  • Identify readiness gaps early
  • Support compliance with training requirements
  • Monitor team-level risk patterns
  • Improve case follow-up and accountability

Export options

Managers can export dashboard data for internal reporting or leadership reviews.
