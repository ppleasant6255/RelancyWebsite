HRIS Synchronization Patterns

How Relancy synchronizes employee and organizational data with HRIS systems.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

HRIS systems serve as the system of record for employee and organizational data. Relancy synchronizes with HRIS platforms to ensure accurate profiles, roles, departments, and reporting structures.

Synchronization methods

  • API‑based data ingestion
  • Scheduled CSV imports
  • Webhook‑triggered updates

Commonly synchronized fields

  • Name and employee ID
  • Role and department
  • Manager relationships
  • Location
  • Status (active, leave, terminated)

Conflict resolution

HRIS is treated as the authoritative source. Conflicts are resolved by applying HRIS values unless overridden by policy.

Audit logging

All synchronization events are logged for transparency and compliance.
