Engine Overview

The Case Load Balancing Engine assigns cases to investigators using workload metrics, expertise profiles, availability, and conflict‑of‑interest safeguards.

Balancing factors

  • Current investigator workload
  • Case category expertise
  • Location and department alignment
  • Conflict‑of‑interest checks
  • Historical performance metrics

Operational impact

Balanced workloads improve investigation quality, reduce delays, and support investigator well‑being.

Audit integrity

Assignment decisions and contributing factors are logged for defensibility and compliance review.
