Workflow Overview

Policy distribution ensures that employees receive, review, and acknowledge updated policies. Distribution is automated, targeted, and fully auditable.

Distribution stages

  • Policy version creation
  • Target audience selection
  • Delivery via email or in‑app notification
  • Employee acknowledgment tracking
  • Compliance reporting

Operational safeguards

Employees cannot access outdated policies. Acknowledgments require explicit confirmation and cannot be auto‑completed.

Audit integrity

Distribution events include timestamp, actor, version metadata, and acknowledgment status for defensibility.
