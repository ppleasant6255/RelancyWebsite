Location Risk Profiling

How Relancy evaluates risk levels across physical locations to support proactive safety and compliance.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Location risk profiling helps organizations identify high‑risk sites based on incident history, readiness gaps, and environmental factors. Relancy aggregates data to produce risk scores for each location.

Risk factors

  • Incident frequency
  • Case severity distribution
  • Training compliance rates
  • Environmental or operational hazards
  • Shift or staffing patterns

Risk scoring

Relancy calculates a composite risk score for each location, updated continuously as new data arrives.

Operational benefits

  • Targeted training
  • Focused safety interventions
  • Resource allocation
  • Leadership reporting

Audit and transparency

All risk calculations are based on immutable audit and lifecycle data.
