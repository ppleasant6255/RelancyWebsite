Device Trust Overview

Device trust policies ensure that only compliant, secure devices can access Relancy. Policies may be enforced through identity providers, MDM systems, or tenant‑defined rules.

Trust requirements

  • OS version compliance
  • MDM enrollment
  • Disk encryption enabled
  • Active antivirus or endpoint protection
  • Jailbreak/root detection

Operational impact

Non‑compliant devices may be blocked or restricted to limited functionality depending on tenant policy.

Audit integrity

Device trust failures and enforcement actions are logged for security and compliance review.
