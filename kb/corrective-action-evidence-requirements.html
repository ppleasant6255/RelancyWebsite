Corrective Action Evidence Requirements

How Relancy defines evidence requirements for corrective action completion and verification.

Back to Knowledge Base

Requirements Overview

Corrective actions require evidence to validate completion. Evidence may include documents, photos, training certificates, or system‑generated confirmations.

Evidence types

  • Document uploads
  • Photo or video proof
  • Training completion records
  • Manager attestations
  • System‑generated logs

Operational safeguards

Evidence must be reviewed and approved by a verifier. Sensitive evidence requires elevated permissions.

Audit integrity

Evidence submissions and approvals are logged with timestamp, actor, and chain‑of‑custody metadata.
