Notification Preferences Configuration

How users configure their notification preferences and delivery channel settings in Relancy.

Back to Knowledge Base

Preferences Overview

Relancy allows users to control which notifications they receive and through which channels. Preferences are scoped per user and enforced within tenant and role boundaries.

Configurable preferences

  • Enable or disable notifications by event type (case updates, escalations, assignments)
  • Choose delivery channels: in-app, email, SMS, or push notification
  • Set quiet hours to suppress non-critical notifications during off-hours
  • Configure digest mode to receive batched summaries instead of individual alerts
  • Override preferences for high-severity events (always delivered regardless of settings)

Channel behavior

  • In-app notifications appear in the notification bell and activity feed
  • Email notifications use tenant-branded templates
  • SMS notifications are limited to critical alerts and require phone verification
  • Push notifications are available on MAUI mobile apps with device registration

Administrative controls

  • Tenant admins can enforce mandatory notifications for compliance-critical events
  • Role-based defaults can be configured so new users inherit appropriate settings
  • Notification preferences are included in user profile exports

Audit integrity

Preference changes and notification delivery attempts are logged with full user, tenant, and timestamp context to support compliance and troubleshooting.
