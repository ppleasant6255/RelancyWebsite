Scoring Overview

Policy Compliance Scoring evaluates how well employees, departments, and locations adhere to organizational policies. Scores combine acknowledgments, training, corrective actions, and case findings.

Scoring components

  • Policy acknowledgment completion
  • Training compliance
  • Corrective action follow‑through
  • Case outcome alignment with policy
  • Behavioral indicators

Operational impact

Compliance scores support audits, leadership reporting, and targeted interventions for at‑risk teams.

Audit integrity

Scoring events and contributing factors are logged for transparency and regulatory alignment.
