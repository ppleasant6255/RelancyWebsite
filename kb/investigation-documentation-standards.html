Overview

Investigations require clear, structured documentation to ensure fairness, accuracy, and legal defensibility. Relancy provides tools to support consistent investigative practice across HR and Security teams.

Required documentation

Every investigation should include:

  • Scope and purpose
  • Timeline of events
  • Interviews and witness statements
  • Evidence collected
  • Findings and analysis
  • Recommendations
  • Final summary

Documentation principles

  • Objectivity: Focus on facts and evidence.
  • Clarity: Use precise, unambiguous language.
  • Completeness: Capture all relevant information.
  • Consistency: Follow the same structure for every investigation.

Evidence handling

All evidence must be uploaded through Relancy’s secure attachment system. Metadata and audit logs ensure integrity and traceability.

Audit and lifecycle

Investigation stages are enforced by the platform. All transitions, edits, and findings are logged in the audit trail.
