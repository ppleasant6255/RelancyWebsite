Module Overview

The Training module centralizes training assignments, completions, expiration cycles, and compliance reporting. It integrates with readiness scoring and role‑based requirements.

Core capabilities

  • Training assignment automation
  • Completion tracking
  • Expiration forecasting
  • Training lineage history
  • External LMS integration

Training types supported

  • Online modules
  • Instructor‑led sessions
  • Policy acknowledgments
  • Certification‑based training

Compliance alignment

Training completions feed directly into readiness scoring and compliance dashboards.

Audit logging

All training events are logged, including assignments, completions, and expirations.
