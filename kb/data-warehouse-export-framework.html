Framework Overview

The Data Warehouse Export Framework provides structured, scheduled, and secure exports of tenant data for use in BI tools and analytics platforms. Exports follow strict privacy and compliance rules.

Export types

  • Incident datasets
  • Case datasets
  • Training and readiness datasets
  • Corrective action datasets
  • Risk and trend datasets

Delivery options

  • SFTP delivery
  • Cloud storage delivery (Azure, AWS, GCP)
  • API‑based retrieval

Privacy safeguards

Sensitive fields may be redacted or anonymized depending on tenant policy. Exports are tenant‑scoped and encrypted in transit and at rest.

Audit integrity

All exports are logged with timestamp, actor, filters, and delivery method for compliance and historical review.
