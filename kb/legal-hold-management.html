Legal Hold Overview

Legal holds prevent the deletion or alteration of data relevant to litigation, investigations, or regulatory inquiries. Holds override retention and purge rules.

Hold capabilities

  • Entity-level holds (cases, incidents, employees)
  • Module-wide holds
  • Tenant-wide holds
  • Automatic suspension of purge processes

Operational safeguards

Data under legal hold is locked from modification and flagged in audit logs for visibility.

Audit integrity

Legal hold creation, modification, and release events are logged for compliance and defensibility.
