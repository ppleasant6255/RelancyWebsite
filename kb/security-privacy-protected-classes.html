Security, Privacy, and Protected Classes

How Relancy safeguards sensitive information and enforces strict privacy boundaries.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy is designed to handle sensitive operational data, including information related to HR, safety, investigations, and protected classes. The platform enforces strict privacy boundaries to ensure that sensitive data is handled responsibly and in compliance with organizational and legal requirements.

Protected-class awareness

Relancy recognizes and safeguards protected-class information such as:

  • Race and ethnicity
  • Gender and gender identity
  • Age
  • Disability status
  • Veteran status
  • Other legally protected categories

This information is handled with heightened sensitivity and is never exposed through unsafe surfaces.

Access control

Access to sensitive data is governed by:

  • Role-based permissions
  • Need-to-know access
  • Audit logging of all access attempts

Unauthorized access is prevented at the platform level.

Data minimization

Relancy collects only the information necessary to support operational workflows. Sensitive fields are restricted, encrypted, and excluded from general reporting unless explicitly required.

Audit and traceability

Every access, update, and lifecycle transition involving sensitive data is logged with:

  • Timestamp
  • Actor
  • Action
  • Context

This ensures a defensible record for compliance and internal review.

Data isolation

Protected-class data is isolated per tenant and never co-mingled. This prevents accidental exposure and simplifies compliance with privacy regulations.
