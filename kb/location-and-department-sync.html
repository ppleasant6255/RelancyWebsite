Location & Department Sync

How Relancy synchronizes location and department structures from HRIS systems to support routing, reporting, and risk modeling.

Sync Overview

Location and department sync ensures that organizational structures remain accurate across Relancy. Syncs update names, codes, hierarchies, and risk profiles.

Synced elements

  • Location names and codes
  • Department names and codes
  • Hierarchy relationships
  • Risk profile mappings
  • Leadership assignments

Operational safeguards

Structural changes trigger updates to routing, dashboards, and risk models. Deprecated locations remain available for historical records.

Audit integrity

Sync events include timestamp, source system, changed fields, and validation results for defensibility.
