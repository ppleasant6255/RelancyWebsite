Interview Overview

Interviews are a critical component of investigations. Relancy provides structured tools for planning, conducting, and documenting interviews while maintaining confidentiality and audit integrity.

Interview components

  • Interview subject and role
  • Scheduled date and interviewer
  • Interview notes and transcripts
  • Linked evidence and attachments
  • Confidentiality indicators

Confidentiality rules

Interview content is restricted to authorized HR or Security roles. Sensitive interviews may require elevated permissions or legal oversight.

Audit logging

All interview actions—including creation, edits, and access—are logged for defensibility and compliance.
