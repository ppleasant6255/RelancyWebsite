Case Assignment Routing Rules

How Relancy automatically routes cases to the correct HR, Security, or leadership roles.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Routing rules ensure that cases are assigned to the correct personnel based on category, severity, location, and organizational policy. Relancy automates routing to reduce delays and improve consistency.

Routing criteria

  • Case category
  • Severity level
  • Department or location
  • Protected‑class involvement
  • Role‑based responsibilities

Routing actions

  • Assign to HR or Security
  • Escalate to leadership
  • Notify managers
  • Trigger follow‑up workflows

Manual overrides

Authorized users may reassign cases when necessary. Overrides are logged with justification.

Audit logging

All routing decisions are logged for transparency and compliance.
