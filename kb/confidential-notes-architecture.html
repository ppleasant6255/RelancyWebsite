Confidential Notes Architecture

How Relancy stores and protects confidential notes using strict access controls, encryption, and audit logging.

Back to Knowledge Base

Architecture Overview

Confidential notes allow investigators and HR/Security personnel to document sensitive information that should not be visible to general case participants or managers.

Confidentiality rules

  • Visible only to authorized roles
  • Encrypted at rest with restricted keys
  • Excluded from standard exports
  • Included only in privileged compliance exports

Operational safeguards

Confidential notes cannot be edited after creation without justification. Redaction requires elevated permissions.

Audit integrity

All note creation, access, and modification events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification.
