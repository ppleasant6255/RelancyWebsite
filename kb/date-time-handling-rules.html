Date/Time Overview

Relancy uses standardized date and time handling rules to ensure consistency across incidents, cases, investigations, training, readiness, and reporting.

Handling rules

  • UTC storage for all timestamps
  • Local time zone display for users
  • ISO‑8601 formatting for exports
  • Consistent date parsing across modules
  • Strict validation for user‑entered dates

Operational impact

Standardized date/time handling prevents misinterpretation, supports global operations, and ensures audit consistency.

Audit integrity

All date/time changes and conversions are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
