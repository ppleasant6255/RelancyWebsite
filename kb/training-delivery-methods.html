Training Delivery Methods

How training is delivered, tracked, and recorded in Relancy.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy supports multiple training delivery methods to accommodate different organizational needs. All training activity is recorded in training lineage to ensure accurate readiness tracking.

Supported delivery methods

  • Online modules: Delivered through integrated or external LMS systems.
  • Instructor-led sessions: Classroom or virtual sessions recorded manually or via import.
  • Video-based training: Hosted content with completion tracking.
  • Policy acknowledgments: Document review and acknowledgment workflows.

Completion tracking

Completion data is captured through:

  • Webhook ingestion from external LMS systems
  • Manual entry by authorized users
  • Automated tracking for integrated modules

Readiness integration

All training completions feed into readiness scoring and organizational readiness dashboards.

Audit and lineage

Every training assignment, completion, expiration, and renewal is recorded in immutable training lineage.
