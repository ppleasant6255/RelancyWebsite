Routing Overview

The Routing Engine evaluates category, severity, location, and organizational structure to determine the correct destination for incidents, cases, and corrective actions. Routing ensures timely review and operational consistency.

Routing inputs

  • Category and subcategory
  • Severity level
  • Location and department
  • Employee role and reporting line
  • Policy‑defined routing rules

Routing outputs

  • HR assignment
  • Security assignment
  • Manager notification
  • Leadership escalation
  • Corrective action creation

Audit integrity

All routing decisions are logged with timestamp, actor, and triggering conditions for defensibility and compliance alignment.
