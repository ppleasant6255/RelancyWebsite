Engine Overview

The Training Assignment Engine evaluates role requirements, readiness gaps, incident involvement, and corrective actions to assign training automatically. Assignments ensure compliance and operational preparedness.

Assignment triggers

  • Role‑based training requirements
  • Readiness score thresholds
  • Incident involvement
  • Corrective action requirements
  • Policy updates

Assignment rules

  • Training is assigned automatically when triggers are met
  • Due dates are calculated based on policy
  • Managers are notified of overdue training
  • Completion updates readiness scores

Audit integrity

All training assignments, completions, and expirations are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
