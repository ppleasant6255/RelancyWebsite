Case Categories and Tagging

How to classify cases for reporting, analysis, and operational clarity.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Categories and tags help organizations classify cases consistently and analyze trends across departments, locations, and time periods. Relancy provides structured and customizable classification tools.

Case categories

Categories represent high‑level classifications such as:

  • Safety
  • Conduct
  • Harassment
  • Security
  • Facilities
  • Policy violation

Tags

Tags provide flexible, multi‑dimensional classification. Examples include:

  • Shift or time period
  • Equipment involved
  • Environmental factors
  • Behavioral indicators
  • Location‑specific identifiers

Reporting benefits

  • Trend analysis
  • Risk pattern detection
  • Department‑level insights
  • Compliance reporting

Audit and consistency

Category and tag changes are logged in the audit trail to preserve historical accuracy.
