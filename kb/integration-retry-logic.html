Retry Overview

Integration retry logic ensures that transient failures—such as network issues or temporary service outages—do not cause data loss or workflow disruption.

Retry rules

  • Exponential backoff with jitter
  • Maximum retry thresholds
  • Automatic escalation to dead‑letter queue
  • Tenant‑scoped retry isolation

Operational safeguards

Retries are rate‑limited and monitored to prevent runaway loops or integration overload.

Audit integrity

Retry attempts, failures, and final outcomes are logged for compliance and operational review.
