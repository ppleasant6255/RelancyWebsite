Marketplace Overview

The Third‑Party App Marketplace provides a curated catalog of integrations that extend Relancy’s capabilities. Apps follow strict security, privacy, and compliance requirements.

Marketplace features

  • Pre‑approved integrations
  • Tenant‑controlled installation
  • Secure OAuth authorization flows
  • Usage analytics and monitoring

Operational safeguards

Apps run in isolated contexts and cannot access data outside their granted scopes. All permissions require explicit tenant approval.

Audit integrity

App installations, permission grants, and data access events are logged for compliance and tenant oversight.
