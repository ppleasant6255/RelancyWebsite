Case Closure Standards

How Relancy ensures cases are closed consistently, completely, and defensibly.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Case closure is a structured process that ensures all required steps have been completed. Relancy enforces closure standards to maintain audit integrity and operational consistency.

Closure requirements

  • All lifecycle stages completed
  • Investigation findings documented
  • Corrective actions completed or assigned
  • Notes and evidence reviewed
  • Severity and category confirmed

Closure review

Some cases may require leadership or HR review before closure, depending on severity or policy.

Audit logging

Case closure is logged with timestamp, actor, and justification.

Reopening cases

Authorized users may reopen cases when new information emerges. Reopen events are logged in the audit trail.
