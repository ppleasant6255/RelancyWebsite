Triage Overview

Intake triage evaluates new incidents to determine urgency, routing, and required safeguards. Triage rules are deterministic and fully auditable.

Triage factors

  • Incident category
  • Severity indicators
  • Location risk profile
  • Reporter safety considerations
  • Protected‑class involvement

Operational safeguards

High‑risk incidents bypass standard queues and route directly to elevated HR/Security roles.

Audit integrity

Triage decisions include timestamp, actor, and contributing factors for defensibility and compliance alignment.
