Module Overview

The HR module provides structured tools for managing employee relations cases, policy violations, protected‑class concerns, and sensitive documentation. It enforces strict RBAC boundaries and audit integrity.

Core capabilities

  • HR case creation and classification
  • Protected notes and restricted access
  • Employee timeline integration
  • Corrective action workflows
  • Training and readiness alignment

Case types handled

  • Conduct issues
  • Harassment and discrimination
  • Policy violations
  • Attendance and performance concerns

Audit and compliance

All HR activity is logged with full audit detail, supporting internal reviews and regulatory compliance.

Access boundaries

Sensitive HR cases are visible only to authorized HR roles. Managers may see limited information depending on policy.
