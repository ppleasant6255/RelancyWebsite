Routing Overview

Sensitive cases—such as harassment, discrimination, violence, or protected‑class matters—are routed using elevated rules to ensure confidentiality, neutrality, and compliance.

Routing criteria

  • Case category and severity
  • Protected‑class involvement
  • Conflict‑of‑interest detection
  • Investigator clearance level
  • Location and reporting line considerations

Operational safeguards

Sensitive cases bypass standard routing and require explicit approval for reassignment or visibility changes.

Audit integrity

Routing decisions and justification are logged for defensibility and regulatory alignment.
