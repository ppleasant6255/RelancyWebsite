Health Score Overview

The Tenant Health Score provides a composite rating of tenant performance, usage patterns, and operational stability. Scores help administrators identify issues and optimize configuration.

Score components

  • API usage and error rates
  • Notification delivery success
  • Background job performance
  • Integration reliability
  • Data quality and validation errors
  • User activity and adoption metrics

Operational impact

Low health scores may indicate configuration issues, integration failures, or training needs for tenant administrators.

Audit integrity

Health score calculations and contributing metrics are logged for transparency and historical review.
