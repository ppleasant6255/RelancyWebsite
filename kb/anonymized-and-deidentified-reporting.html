Reporting Overview

Relancy supports anonymized and de‑identified reporting to protect employee privacy while enabling trend analysis and risk monitoring. These reports remove or mask personally identifiable information.

De‑identification methods

  • Removal of names and employee IDs
  • Aggregation of small groups
  • Location and department generalization
  • Masking of sensitive fields

Use cases

  • Leadership dashboards
  • Safety committee reviews
  • Quarterly risk assessments
  • Policy effectiveness evaluations

Governance

Administrators define which fields may be included in anonymized reports and configure thresholds for aggregation.

Audit logging

Even anonymized exports are logged to maintain transparency and compliance.
