Dashboard Overview

The Compliance Dashboard consolidates key indicators across training, readiness, incidents, and corrective actions. It supports regulatory alignment, audit preparation, and proactive compliance management.

Key metrics

  • Training completion rates
  • Readiness score distribution
  • Incident and case volume trends
  • Corrective action performance
  • Policy adherence indicators

Compliance alerts

Alerts highlight overdue training, expiring certifications, high‑risk locations, and unresolved corrective actions. Alerts may trigger escalations based on policy.

Reporting integration

Compliance dashboards feed into audit reports, leadership reviews, and regulatory submissions.

Access boundaries

Only authorized compliance roles may view sensitive compliance data. Aggregated or anonymized views may be provided to leadership or safety committees.
