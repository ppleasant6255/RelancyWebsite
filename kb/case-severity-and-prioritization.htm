Case Severity and Prioritization

How Relancy classifies and prioritizes operational cases.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Severity and prioritization help organizations respond to cases with the appropriate urgency. Relancy provides a structured severity model that ensures consistent classification and clear expectations for response.

Severity levels

Relancy uses a standard severity scale:

  • Severity 1 – Critical: Immediate threat to safety, compliance, or operations.
  • Severity 2 – High: Significant impact requiring prompt action.
  • Severity 3 – Medium: Moderate impact requiring timely review.
  • Severity 4 – Low: Minimal impact; routine handling.

Prioritization factors

Severity is determined by factors such as:

  • Risk to individuals
  • Operational disruption
  • Regulatory exposure
  • Reputational impact
  • Historical patterns or repeat behavior

Lifecycle integration

Severity influences escalation rules, notification behavior, and required lifecycle steps. Critical cases may require immediate assignment and multi-role involvement.

Audit and reporting

Severity changes are logged in the audit trail and reflected in reporting dashboards for trend analysis.
