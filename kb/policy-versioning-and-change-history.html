Versioning Overview

Policy versioning ensures that organizations can track changes over time, maintain historical records, and validate employee acknowledgments for each version.

Versioning capabilities

  • Automatic version creation on update
  • Side-by-side version comparison
  • Employee acknowledgment tracking per version
  • Retention of historical versions

Operational impact

Versioning supports audits, legal reviews, and compliance reporting by providing a clear history of policy evolution.

Audit integrity

All policy changes and acknowledgments are logged with timestamp, actor, and version metadata.
