Case Reopen Criteria and Governance

How Relancy governs the reopening of closed cases to maintain audit integrity and operational clarity.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Cases may need to be reopened when new information emerges or corrective actions require follow‑up. Relancy enforces governance rules to ensure reopen events are justified and fully auditable.

Valid reopen scenarios

  • New evidence becomes available
  • Incorrect closure due to user error
  • Corrective actions not completed
  • Leadership or legal request

Authorization

Only authorized HR or Security administrators may reopen cases.

Audit logging

Reopen events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification. Historical lifecycle data remains intact.

Operational impact

Reopened cases re‑enter the lifecycle and may trigger new assignments, escalations, or corrective actions.
