Governance Overview

Data exports are tightly governed to protect sensitive information and maintain compliance. Relancy enforces strict RBAC, audit logging, and export‑level redaction rules.

Export types

  • Incident and case exports
  • Training and readiness exports
  • Corrective action exports
  • Location and department reports
  • Audit log exports (restricted)

Access control

Only authorized roles may export data. Sensitive fields may be automatically redacted depending on policy.

Compliance alignment

Export formats and retention rules align with regulatory requirements and internal audit standards.

Audit logging

All exports are logged with timestamp, actor, filters used, and file type.
