HR/Security Case Reassignment Rules

How Relancy governs case reassignment between HR and Security roles while preserving audit integrity and confidentiality.

Reassignment Overview

Case reassignment ensures that cases are handled by the correct HR or Security roles based on category, severity, and organizational policy. Reassignments are tightly controlled and fully auditable.

Reassignment triggers

  • Incorrect initial assignment
  • Category or severity change
  • Conflict of interest
  • Workload balancing
  • Organizational structure changes

Reassignment rules

  • Only authorized HR or Security roles may reassign cases
  • Reassignments require justification
  • Confidentiality boundaries must be preserved
  • All related tasks and notifications follow the new owner

Audit integrity

All reassignment events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification for compliance and defensibility.
