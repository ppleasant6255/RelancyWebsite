Routing Overview

Relancy uses structured routing rules to ensure incidents and cases reach the correct HR, Security, or leadership roles. Routing is based on category, severity, location, and organizational structure.

Escalation triggers

  • High‑severity incidents
  • Protected‑class involvement
  • Repeat behavior indicators
  • Overdue corrective actions
  • Policy‑defined categories

Routing paths

Routing paths may include HR, Security, local managers, regional leaders, or compliance teams depending on policy and severity.

Override rules

Authorized users may override routing when necessary. Overrides require justification and are fully audited.

Audit logging

All routing and escalation events are logged with timestamp, actor, and triggering conditions.
