Correlation Overview

Cross‑Module Risk Correlation identifies relationships between incidents, case outcomes, readiness scores, training gaps, and behavioral patterns to reveal systemic risks.

Correlation factors

  • Incident clusters linked to readiness gaps
  • Case outcomes correlated with training deficiencies
  • Location‑level risk patterns
  • Behavioral indicators tied to corrective actions
  • Trend alignment across modules

Operational impact

Correlation insights support strategic planning, targeted interventions, and compliance reporting.

Audit integrity

Correlation events and underlying datasets are logged for transparency and defensibility.
