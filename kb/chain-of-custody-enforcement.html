Chain‑of‑Custody Overview

Chain‑of‑custody ensures that evidence remains unaltered and traceable from the moment it is captured. Relancy enforces strict controls to maintain evidentiary integrity for HR, Security, and legal review.

Custody events

  • Evidence upload
  • Evidence access or viewing
  • Evidence transfer between owners
  • Redaction or annotation
  • Administrative overrides

Integrity safeguards

  • Immutable audit logs
  • Cryptographic hashing of evidence files
  • Restricted access based on RBAC
  • Versioning for redacted or annotated evidence

Compliance alignment

Chain‑of‑custody rules align with legal standards for workplace investigations and regulatory requirements.
