Export Redaction Rules

How Relancy redacts or anonymizes sensitive fields during data exports to protect privacy and comply with policy.

Redaction Overview

Export redaction rules ensure that sensitive or confidential information is removed or anonymized before data is delivered to BI tools, data warehouses, or external systems.

Redacted fields

  • Protected‑class indicators
  • Confidential notes
  • Witness identities
  • Reporter identities (when anonymous)
  • Sensitive HR or Security details

Anonymization rules

Identifiers may be hashed or replaced with surrogate keys depending on tenant policy and export type.

Audit integrity

Redaction rules and export logs are retained for compliance and defensibility.
