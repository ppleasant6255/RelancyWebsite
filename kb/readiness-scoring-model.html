Readiness Scoring Model

How Relancy calculates individual and organizational readiness.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Readiness is Relancy’s measure of whether individuals and teams are prepared to respond to incidents, investigations, and operational responsibilities. The readiness score is derived from training lineage, role requirements, and organizational policy.

Inputs to readiness scoring

  • Training assignments
  • Completion dates
  • Expiration dates
  • Renewal history
  • Role-based requirements

Scoring logic

Readiness is calculated using a weighted model that considers:

  • Mandatory vs optional training
  • Recency of completion
  • Upcoming expirations
  • Overdue items

Readiness states

  • Ready: All required training is current.
  • At Risk: One or more items are nearing expiration.
  • Not Ready: One or more required items are overdue.

Organizational readiness

Relancy aggregates individual readiness into team, department, and organization-wide metrics. These dashboards help leaders identify gaps and take proactive action.
