Glossary of Core Relancy Terms

Definitions of key concepts used throughout the Relancy platform.

Back to Knowledge Base

Case

The central operational entity that unifies incidents, investigations, training, and readiness into a single narrative.

Incident

A reported event requiring validation, review, or operational response. Incidents may be linked to cases.

Investigation

A structured process for gathering facts, analyzing findings, and making recommendations.

Training Lineage

The immutable record of all training assignments, completions, expirations, and renewals.

Readiness

A measure of whether individuals or teams are prepared to fulfill operational responsibilities.

Lifecycle

The sequence of states an entity moves through, enforced by the platform to ensure consistency and audit integrity.

Webhook

A real-time notification sent to an external system when a lifecycle event occurs.

Tenant

An isolated organizational environment within Relancy, with its own data, users, and configuration.

Private Deployment

A dedicated Relancy environment with custom infrastructure, optional write-level APIs, and extended control.
