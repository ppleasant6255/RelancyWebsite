Overview

High‑quality investigations require structure, neutrality, and complete documentation. Relancy enforces standards that help HR and Security teams conduct investigations that withstand internal and external review.

Quality criteria

  • Clear scope and objectives
  • Neutral, fact‑based documentation
  • Complete evidence collection
  • Consistent interview methodology
  • Traceable findings and recommendations

Platform support

  • Structured investigation templates
  • Chain‑of‑custody enforcement
  • Audit logging of all actions
  • Lifecycle controls to prevent skipped steps

Review and approval

Some investigations may require leadership or legal review before closure, depending on severity or policy.

Reporting

Investigation quality metrics appear in dashboards to support continuous improvement.
