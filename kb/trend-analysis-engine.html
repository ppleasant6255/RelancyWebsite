Engine Overview

The Trend Analysis Engine identifies long‑term patterns in organizational behavior, risk, and operational performance. Trends support leadership decision‑making and compliance planning.

Trend categories

  • Incident frequency and severity trends
  • Case outcome patterns
  • Readiness score trajectories
  • Corrective action completion rates
  • Location and department risk shifts

Operational impact

Trends inform training programs, policy updates, and resource allocation across the organization.

Audit integrity

Trend calculations and underlying datasets are logged for transparency and defensibility.
