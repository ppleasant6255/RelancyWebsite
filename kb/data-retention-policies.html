Retention Overview

Data retention policies define how long incidents, cases, evidence, training records, and audit logs are stored. Retention ensures compliance with legal, regulatory, and organizational requirements.

Retention categories

  • Short-term operational data
  • Long-term compliance data
  • Immutable audit logs
  • Tenant-configurable retention windows

Operational safeguards

Data scheduled for deletion is queued, validated, and purged using secure, irreversible deletion processes.

Audit integrity

Retention policy changes and purge events are logged for compliance and historical review.
