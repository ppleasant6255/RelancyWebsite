Tenant Branding Options

How tenants can customize their Relancy experience with approved branding elements.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy supports limited tenant branding to help organizations align the platform with their internal identity while preserving consistency, accessibility, and platform integrity.

Supported branding elements

  • Tenant logo
  • Accent color selection
  • Custom panels for contextual information

Branding restrictions

To maintain accessibility and platform consistency, Relancy does not allow:

  • Custom fonts
  • Custom layouts
  • Custom CSS or scripts
  • Changes to lifecycle or workflow UI

Private Deployment options

Private Deployment tenants may implement extended branding options, subject to governance and accessibility requirements.

Audit and governance

Branding changes are logged in the audit trail to preserve historical accuracy.
