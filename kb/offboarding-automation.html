Automation Overview

Offboarding automation ensures that terminated employees lose access immediately and that open tasks, cases, and assignments are safely reassigned.

Automation components

  • Immediate access revocation
  • Task and case reassignment
  • Notification to managers and HR
  • Historical record preservation
  • Readiness and training archive

Operational safeguards

Offboarding cannot be reversed without HR approval. Sensitive cases require manual reassignment review.

Audit integrity

Offboarding events include timestamp, actor, and reassignment details for compliance and defensibility.
