Scoring Overview

Intake Quality Scoring evaluates incident submissions for completeness, clarity, and actionable detail. Scores help triage teams prioritize follow‑up and reduce investigation delays.

Scoring factors

  • Completeness of required fields
  • Clarity of narrative
  • Presence of evidence
  • Specificity of location and participants
  • Consistency with category and severity

Operational safeguards

Quality scores never affect reporter credibility. They only guide triage and follow‑up requirements.

Audit integrity

Scoring events and contributing factors are logged for transparency and compliance alignment.
