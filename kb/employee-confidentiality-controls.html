Employee Confidentiality Controls

How Relancy protects sensitive employee information through access controls and audit enforcement.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Confidentiality is essential when handling employee information. Relancy enforces strict access controls, protected fields, and audit logging to safeguard sensitive data.

Confidential data types

  • Protected‑class attributes
  • Medical or accommodation information
  • Sensitive HR case details
  • Security‑related information
  • Witness statements

Access restrictions

Only authorized roles may view or edit confidential data. Some fields may be restricted to HR or Security administrators only.

Audit enforcement

All access to confidential data is logged, including views, edits, and exports.

Reporting boundaries

Confidential data is excluded from general reports unless the user has explicit permissions.

Private Deployment options

Private Deployment tenants may implement custom confidentiality rules or integrate external compliance systems.
