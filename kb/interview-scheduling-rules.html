Scheduling Overview

Interview scheduling ensures that interviews occur promptly, with the right participants, and without conflicts or privacy risks.

Scheduling factors

  • Participant availability
  • Case sensitivity and urgency
  • Conflict‑of‑interest detection
  • Location and time zone alignment
  • Investigator workload

Operational safeguards

Sensitive interviews require private meeting links and restricted calendar visibility.

Audit integrity

Scheduling events include timestamp, actor, and justification for any overrides or rescheduling.
