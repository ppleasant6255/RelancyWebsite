How Relancy manages tenant‑defined vocabularies for categories, tags, labels, and classification fields.

Vocabulary Overview

Controlled vocabularies ensure consistent classification across incidents, cases, training, and readiness. Tenants may define their own vocabularies within safe, governed boundaries.

Vocabulary types

  • Incident categories
  • Case categories
  • Tag libraries
  • Training categories
  • Corrective action types

Operational safeguards

  • Vocabularies cannot conflict with core system categories
  • Changes propagate safely across modules
  • Deprecated terms remain available for historical records

Audit integrity

Vocabulary changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and previous values for compliance and defensibility.
