Verification Overview

Training completion requires module completion, passing assessments, and—when applicable—evidence submission. Verification ensures compliance and readiness accuracy.

Verification components

  • Assessment score validation
  • Completion timestamp confirmation
  • Evidence review (if required)
  • Manager or HR approval
  • Readiness score update

Operational safeguards

Verification cannot be overridden without justification. Failed assessments require retakes.

Audit integrity

Verification events are logged with timestamp, actor, and evidence references for compliance alignment.
