Engine Overview

The Duplicate Detection Engine prevents redundant incident creation by comparing new submissions against recent reports using structured similarity rules.

Detection factors

  • Location and timestamp proximity
  • Category and subcategory match
  • Keyword and narrative similarity
  • Involved employee overlap
  • Behavioral pattern alignment

Operational safeguards

Duplicate suggestions never auto‑merge. Investigators must confirm or reject duplicates with justification.

Audit integrity

All duplicate detection events and investigator decisions are logged for transparency and defensibility.
