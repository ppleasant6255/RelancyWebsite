Location Management Overview

Locations represent physical sites, offices, facilities, or operational areas within your organization. Relancy uses locations as a core organizational dimension that connects employees, incidents, cases, risk profiles, and compliance requirements.

Location data model

  • Location name, address, and geographic coordinates
  • Location type (office, warehouse, field site, remote)
  • Parent-child hierarchy for multi-level site structures
  • Associated department and cost center mappings
  • Active/inactive status with effective dates

Location lifecycle

  • Create locations with full address and classification details
  • Assign employees and departments to locations
  • Transfer employees between locations with workflow approvals
  • Deactivate locations while preserving historical data and associations
  • View location timeline showing all events and changes over time

Risk and compliance integration

  • Each location has a risk profile based on incident history and environmental factors
  • Location risk scores feed into the AIM readiness dashboard
  • Compliance requirements can be location-specific (e.g., state regulations)
  • Incident trends are analyzed per location for pattern detection

Audit integrity

All location changes, transfers, deactivations, and risk profile updates are logged with full tenant, user, and timestamp context.
