User Preferences and Personalization

How users customize their Relancy experience through preferences and personal settings.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy allows users to personalize aspects of their experience while maintaining consistent platform behavior. Preferences are stored per user and synchronized across devices.

Available preferences

  • Default dashboard view
  • Notification settings
  • Theme and accessibility options
  • Preferred filters and sorting
  • Language and locale

Persistence

Preferences are saved automatically and applied across sessions and devices.

RBAC boundaries

Personalization does not override role‑based access or visibility rules.
