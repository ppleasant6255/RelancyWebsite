Legal Hold and Data Preservation

How Relancy preserves data when legal or regulatory requirements mandate retention.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Legal holds require organizations to preserve specific data until legal or regulatory matters are resolved. Relancy enforces legal hold rules to prevent deletion or modification of relevant records.

Legal hold triggers

  • Pending litigation
  • Regulatory investigations
  • Internal compliance reviews
  • Leadership or legal department request

Preservation rules

  • Prevent deletion of cases, incidents, or evidence
  • Restrict editing of sensitive records
  • Preserve audit logs indefinitely

Visibility

Legal hold status is visible only to authorized HR, Security, and legal personnel.

Audit logging

All legal hold actions are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification.
