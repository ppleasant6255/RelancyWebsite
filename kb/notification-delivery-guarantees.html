Delivery Overview

Relancy uses redundant delivery channels, retry logic, and delivery confirmation to ensure that notifications reach the intended recipients. Delivery guarantees vary by channel based on technical constraints.

Email delivery guarantees

  • At‑least‑once delivery with retry logic
  • Fallback routing for transient failures
  • Delivery status tracking
  • Tenant‑branded templates

SMS delivery guarantees

SMS delivery depends on carrier networks. Relancy retries failed messages and logs delivery confirmations when available. Sensitive content is excluded from SMS.

In‑app delivery guarantees

  • Guaranteed delivery when the user is online
  • Queued delivery for offline users
  • Persistent notifications until dismissed

Audit integrity

All notification events—including delivery attempts, failures, and confirmations—are logged for compliance and operational review.
