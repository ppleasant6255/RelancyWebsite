Overview

Recurrence tracking helps organizations identify patterns of repeated incidents. Relancy automatically detects recurrence across individuals, locations, and categories.

Recurrence indicators

  • Multiple incidents involving the same employee
  • Repeated issues at the same location
  • Recurring case categories
  • Patterns across shifts or departments

Operational impact

  • Triggers risk notifications
  • May escalate severity
  • Supports root cause analysis
  • Informs corrective actions

Reporting

Recurrence patterns appear in dashboards and exports for leadership review.

Audit integrity

Recurrence detection is based on immutable incident and case history.
