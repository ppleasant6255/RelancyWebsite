Case Lifecycle Exceptions

How Relancy handles rare situations where standard lifecycle rules require administrative override.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy enforces strict lifecycle rules to maintain audit integrity. However, certain rare situations may require administrative exceptions. These exceptions are tightly controlled and fully audited.

Valid exception scenarios

  • Incorrect case type selected at creation
  • Misassigned lifecycle stage due to user error
  • Duplicate cases requiring consolidation
  • Legal or compliance‑mandated adjustments

Who can apply exceptions

Only authorized administrators (typically HR or Security leads) may apply lifecycle exceptions.

Audit logging

All exceptions are logged with timestamp, actor, justification, and the original lifecycle state.

Restrictions

Exceptions cannot remove required lifecycle steps or alter historical audit entries.
