Case Lifecycle Analytics

Overview

Case lifecycle analytics provide insight into how efficiently cases move through required stages. Relancy tracks timing, bottlenecks, and compliance with lifecycle rules to support continuous improvement.

Key lifecycle metrics

  • Time spent in each lifecycle stage
  • Total time to resolution
  • Stage‑level SLA performance
  • Escalation frequency
  • Reopen rate

Bottleneck detection

Relancy identifies stages where cases frequently stall, helping organizations target process improvements or staffing adjustments.

Compliance alignment

Lifecycle analytics support internal audits and regulatory reviews by demonstrating adherence to required workflows.

Reporting

Analytics appear in dashboards and exports, enabling leadership to monitor performance across teams and locations.
