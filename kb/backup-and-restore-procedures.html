Backup & Restore Procedures

How Relancy performs secure backups and restores tenant data while maintaining isolation and audit integrity.

Back to Knowledge Base

Backup Overview

Relancy performs automated, encrypted backups of tenant data to ensure recoverability in the event of data loss or corruption. Backups follow strict retention and isolation rules.

Backup rules

  • Daily full backups
  • Hourly incremental backups
  • Encrypted storage
  • Tenant‑scoped isolation
  • Retention based on tenant policy

Restore procedures

Restores are performed on request or during DR events. Restores maintain tenant isolation and preserve audit history.

Audit integrity

Backup and restore events are logged with timestamp, actor, and affected datasets for compliance review.
