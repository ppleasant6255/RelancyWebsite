Permissions Overview

The mobile app requests only the permissions required for operational workflows. Permissions are optional unless needed for specific features such as evidence capture or offline mode.

Permission types

  • Camera (evidence capture)
  • Microphone (audio evidence)
  • Storage (offline caching)
  • Notifications (alerts and reminders)
  • Location (optional for certain workflows)

Privacy safeguards

Permissions are never used for background tracking or unauthorized data collection. Users may revoke permissions at any time.

Audit integrity

Permission changes and evidence capture events are logged for compliance and operational review.
