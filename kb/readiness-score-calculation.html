Score Overview

Readiness scores measure an employee’s preparedness and compliance posture. Scores combine training completion, behavioral indicators, corrective action history, and risk factors.

Score components

  • Training completion rate
  • Assessment performance
  • Corrective action follow‑through
  • Behavioral pattern indicators
  • Location and department risk multipliers

Operational safeguards

Readiness scores cannot be used for disciplinary decisions without human review. Protected‑class data is excluded.

Audit integrity

Score calculations and contributing factors are logged for transparency and defensibility.
