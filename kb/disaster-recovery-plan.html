DR Overview

The Disaster Recovery Plan ensures that Relancy remains operational during catastrophic events. DR procedures include redundant infrastructure, automated failover, and rapid recovery protocols.

DR components

  • Multi‑region redundancy
  • Automated failover
  • Data replication
  • Backup restoration procedures
  • Regular DR testing

Recovery objectives

  • RPO: Minimal data loss
  • RTO: Rapid service restoration

Audit integrity

DR tests, failover events, and recovery actions are logged for compliance and operational review.
