Schema Overview

The Data Warehouse Schema provides a normalized, analytics‑ready structure for exported Relancy datasets. Schemas are designed for BI tools, data lakes, and enterprise data warehouses.

Core fact tables

  • FactIncidents – incident events, severity, timestamps
  • FactCases – case lifecycle, findings, outcomes
  • FactTraining – assignments, completions, expirations
  • FactReadiness – score changes, weighting factors
  • FactCorrectiveActions – tasks, deadlines, verifications

Dimension tables

  • DimEmployee – role, department, location
  • DimLocation – region, risk profile
  • DimDepartment – hierarchy, functional area
  • DimCategory – incident and case taxonomy
  • DimTime – date/time normalization

Privacy safeguards

Sensitive fields may be redacted or anonymized depending on tenant policy. Protected‑class indicators are excluded unless explicitly enabled.

Audit integrity

Schema versions and export mappings are logged for compliance and historical traceability.
