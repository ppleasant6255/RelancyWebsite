HA Overview

High Availability (HA) architecture ensures that Relancy remains operational even during component failures. HA is achieved through redundancy, distributed services, and automated failover.

HA components

  • Redundant API nodes
  • Distributed databases
  • Load‑balanced services
  • Multi‑region failover
  • Continuous health monitoring

Operational impact

HA ensures minimal downtime, consistent performance, and reliable access for all tenants.

Audit integrity

Failover events and HA system changes are logged for compliance and operational review.
