Linking Overview

Relancy supports structured linking between entities to provide full context and traceability. Links are directional, auditable, and governed by RBAC.

Common link types

  • Incident ↔ Case
  • Case ↔ Investigation
  • Employee ↔ Case or Incident
  • Location ↔ Incident or Case
  • Corrective Action ↔ Root Cause

Operational behavior

Linked entities are surfaced in context panels and timelines, allowing users to navigate across related records without losing their place.

Audit logging

Creation and removal of links are logged, including who made the change and when.
