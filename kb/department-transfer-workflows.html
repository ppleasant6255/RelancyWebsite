Transfer Overview

Department transfers affect training requirements, readiness scores, routing rules, and visibility boundaries. Relancy ensures that department transfers are processed consistently and safely.

Transfer tasks

  • Update department assignment
  • Recalculate readiness score
  • Assign department‑specific training
  • Update routing and visibility rules
  • Notify managers and compliance roles

Operational impact

Department transfers ensure that employees meet functional requirements and that dashboards reflect accurate data.

Audit integrity

All transfer events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification for defensibility.
