Branding Overview

The Tenant Branding & Theme Engine allows organizations to apply their own logos, colors, and visual identity across the Relancy interface. Branding is isolated per tenant and does not affect platform structure.

Branding elements

  • Tenant logo
  • Primary and secondary color palette
  • Button and accent colors
  • Email template branding
  • Login page customization

Operational safeguards

Branding cannot override accessibility requirements, break UI layouts, or introduce unsafe HTML or scripts.

Audit integrity

Branding changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and previous values for compliance and historical review.
