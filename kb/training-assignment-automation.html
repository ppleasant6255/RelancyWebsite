Training Assignment Automation

How Relancy automatically assigns training based on role, department, and readiness requirements.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Automated training assignment ensures employees receive the correct training without manual intervention. Relancy evaluates role, department, and policy requirements to assign training dynamically.

Automation triggers

  • New employee onboarding
  • Role changes
  • Department transfers
  • Location‑specific requirements
  • Training expiration or renewal cycles

Assignment logic

Relancy matches employees to required training modules based on predefined rules and readiness criteria.

Notifications

Employees and managers receive alerts when new training is assigned or when deadlines approach.

Audit logging

All automated assignments are logged with timestamp and rule source.
