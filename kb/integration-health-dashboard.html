Dashboard Overview

The Integration Health Dashboard displays the status of all active integrations, including HRIS, LMS, SSO, webhooks, and custom connectors. It supports troubleshooting and operational monitoring.

Key indicators

  • Success and failure rates
  • Retry volume
  • Latency and throughput
  • Endpoint availability
  • Payload validation errors

Operational impact

Administrators can quickly identify failing integrations, misconfigurations, or external system outages.

Audit integrity

Integration health events and dashboard views are logged for compliance and operational review.
