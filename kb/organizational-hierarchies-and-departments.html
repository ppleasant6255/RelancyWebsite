Organizational Hierarchies and Departments

How Relancy models reporting structures and departmental boundaries.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy supports hierarchical organizational structures to enable accurate reporting, readiness tracking, and case assignment. Departments and reporting lines help contextualize incidents, investigations, and training requirements.

Department structure

Departments can be configured to reflect your organization’s structure, such as:

  • HR
  • Security
  • Operations
  • Facilities
  • IT
  • Regional or site-based groups

Reporting hierarchy

Each employee profile includes a manager or supervisor, enabling:

  • Escalation routing
  • Readiness rollups
  • Case visibility for leadership
  • Department-level reporting

Cross-functional collaboration

HR and Security often collaborate on cases. Relancy enforces boundaries through RBAC while enabling shared visibility where appropriate.

Audit and changes

Changes to organizational structure are logged in the audit trail to preserve historical accuracy.
