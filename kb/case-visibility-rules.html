Case Visibility Rules

How Relancy determines who can see which cases, incidents, and investigations.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Case visibility is governed by role-based access control (RBAC), case ownership, and organizational hierarchy. Relancy ensures that sensitive information is visible only to authorized users while supporting collaboration across HR, Security, and leadership.

Visibility factors

  • User role and permissions
  • Case category and sensitivity
  • Department or location alignment
  • Ownership and assignment
  • Protected-class restrictions

Restricted cases

Certain case types (e.g., harassment, discrimination, high-severity safety incidents) may be restricted to specific roles such as HR Administrators or Security Officers.

Leadership visibility

Leaders may have visibility into cases involving their direct reports or departments, depending on policy.

Audit logging

All case views are logged in the audit trail to ensure transparency and accountability.
