Multi-Role Collaboration

How HR, Security, and leadership collaborate safely within Relancy’s boundaries.

Overview

Many cases require collaboration across HR, Security, and leadership. Relancy supports multi-role workflows while enforcing strict access boundaries through RBAC and audit logging.

Collaboration scenarios

  • HR and Security jointly managing a case
  • Leadership reviewing high-severity incidents
  • Training coordinators addressing readiness gaps
  • Managers participating in corrective actions

Access boundaries

Each role sees only the information necessary for their responsibilities. Sensitive data is restricted to authorized roles.

Communication tools

Collaboration occurs through:

  • Case notes
  • Assignments
  • Lifecycle transitions
  • Corrective action workflows

Audit and accountability

All collaborative actions are logged in the audit trail, ensuring transparency and accountability.
