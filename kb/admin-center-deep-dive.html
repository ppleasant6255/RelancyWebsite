Admin Center Overview

The Admin Center provides tenant administrators with tools to configure roles, permissions, policies, taxonomy, and platform behavior. It is the control surface for governance and operational consistency.

Administrative capabilities

  • Role and permission management
  • Incident and case taxonomy configuration
  • Training and readiness requirements
  • Escalation and routing rules
  • Organizational structure management
  • Tenant branding and decoration

Governance controls

Administrators define policies that govern case visibility, lifecycle rules, and confidentiality boundaries.

Audit logging

All administrative actions are logged for compliance and historical traceability.
