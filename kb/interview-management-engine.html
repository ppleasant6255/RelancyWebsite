Engine Overview

The Interview Management Engine orchestrates the full lifecycle of interviews, including scheduling, participant management, note capture, evidence attachments, and audit logging.

Interview components

  • Interview scheduling and reminders
  • Participant role tracking
  • Confidential note capture
  • Evidence and document attachments
  • Outcome and summary recording

Operational safeguards

Interview notes are immutable once finalized. Sensitive interviews require elevated permissions and restricted visibility.

Audit integrity

All interview events—creation, edits, attendance, and outcomes—are logged with timestamp and actor metadata.
