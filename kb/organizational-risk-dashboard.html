Organizational Risk Dashboard

How Relancy visualizes risk patterns across the entire organization.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

The organizational risk dashboard provides leadership with a high‑level view of risk patterns, readiness gaps, and incident trends across all departments and locations.

Dashboard components

  • Incident heatmaps
  • Location risk scores
  • Readiness distribution
  • Case severity trends
  • Corrective action status
  • Protected‑class risk indicators (restricted)

Leadership visibility

Access is restricted to authorized executives and compliance roles. Sensitive data is masked unless permissions allow full visibility.

Operational benefits

  • Proactive risk mitigation
  • Resource allocation
  • Policy refinement
  • Compliance oversight

Export options

Dashboard data can be exported for board reporting or compliance audits.
