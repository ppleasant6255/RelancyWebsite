Index Overview

The Safety Index provides a composite score representing organizational safety health. It combines risk scores, readiness levels, training compliance, and behavioral indicators.

Index components

  • Risk Model Engine output
  • Readiness score averages
  • Training completion rates
  • Corrective action performance
  • Behavioral pattern indicators

Operational impact

The Safety Index supports leadership dashboards, compliance reviews, and strategic planning for safety programs.

Audit integrity

Index calculations and contributing factors are logged for transparency, defensibility, and regulatory alignment.
