Workflow Overview

Post‑investigation reviews evaluate case quality, investigator performance, and adherence to policy and regulatory requirements. Reviews support continuous improvement.

Review components

  • Case timeline evaluation
  • Evidence completeness review
  • Findings and determination quality
  • Corrective action alignment
  • Investigator performance insights

Operational safeguards

Reviews cannot alter case outcomes. Any required corrections must follow formal amendment workflows.

Audit integrity

Review events and findings are logged for transparency, compliance, and leadership oversight.
