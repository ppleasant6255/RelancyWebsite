Case Severity Auto‑Classification

How Relancy automatically assigns severity levels based on case details and organizational policy.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Auto‑classification helps organizations maintain consistent severity assignments across cases. Relancy evaluates case details and applies severity rules defined by administrators.

Auto‑classification factors

  • Incident type
  • Protected‑class involvement
  • Injury or safety risk
  • Repeat behavior
  • Location risk profile
  • Policy‑defined triggers

Manual overrides

Authorized users may adjust severity when appropriate. Overrides are logged with justification.

Notifications

Auto‑classified high‑severity cases may trigger escalations or leadership alerts.

Audit integrity

All auto‑classification events are logged with timestamp and rule source.
