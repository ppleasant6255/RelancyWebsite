Audit Overview

The Communication Audit Log records all messages sent through Relancy, including broadcasts, policy updates, training reminders, and safety alerts.

Logged metadata

  • Sender identity
  • Recipient list
  • Delivery channel
  • Timestamp and status
  • Message content hash
  • Approval chain (if applicable)

Operational safeguards

Message content is hashed for privacy. Sensitive messages include elevated audit detail and retention rules.

Audit integrity

Logs are immutable and retained according to tenant retention, legal hold, and regulatory requirements.
