Notification Overview

Relancy provides in‑app notifications for assignments, escalations, training deadlines, and lifecycle changes. Notifications route users directly to the relevant entity or dashboard.

Notification types

  • Case assignments
  • Incident submissions
  • Investigation updates
  • Training deadlines
  • Corrective action reminders

Routing behavior

Tapping a notification opens the associated case, incident, or dashboard with filters applied.

RBAC enforcement

Users only receive notifications for entities they are authorized to access.

Notification settings

Users may customize notification preferences in their profile settings.
