Heatmap Overview

Location Risk Heatmaps provide visual summaries of risk across geographic or operational locations. Heatmaps combine multiple indicators to highlight high‑risk areas.

Risk inputs

  • Incident severity and frequency
  • Case outcomes and escalation rates
  • Readiness score averages
  • Corrective action performance
  • Behavioral pattern clusters

Operational impact

Heatmaps help leadership prioritize interventions, allocate resources, and monitor regional risk trends.

Audit integrity

Heatmap generation events and underlying datasets are logged for transparency and defensibility.
