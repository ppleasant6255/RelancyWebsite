Workflow Overview

Safety committees use Relancy to review incidents, track corrective actions, and ensure regulatory compliance. The SOC module provides structured workflows that align with OSHA and industry-specific safety standards.

Committee review process

  • Incidents are routed to the safety committee based on severity and type
  • Committee members review incident details, evidence, and investigator findings
  • Voting or consensus-based decisions are recorded with full attribution
  • Corrective actions are assigned with owners, deadlines, and follow-up schedules

Recurring meeting support

  • Schedule recurring committee meetings with agenda templates
  • Auto-populate meeting agendas with pending incidents and open corrective actions
  • Track attendance and quorum requirements
  • Generate meeting minutes with action items

Corrective action tracking

  • Assign corrective actions to specific users with due dates
  • Track completion status and effectiveness reviews
  • Escalate overdue actions to supervisors automatically
  • Link corrective actions back to originating incidents for traceability

Audit integrity

All committee decisions, votes, corrective action assignments, and status changes are logged with full user, tenant, and timestamp context to support regulatory audits.
