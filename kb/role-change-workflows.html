Workflow Overview

Role changes affect training requirements, readiness scores, routing rules, and visibility boundaries. Relancy ensures that role changes are processed consistently and safely.

Role change tasks

  • Update role and permissions
  • Recalculate readiness score
  • Assign new training requirements
  • Reassign cases if necessary
  • Update reporting line visibility

Operational impact

Role changes ensure that employees have the correct access and responsibilities aligned with their new position.

Audit integrity

All role change events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification for defensibility.
