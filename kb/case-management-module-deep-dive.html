Module Overview

The Case Management module provides structured workflows for handling HR and Security cases. It enforces lifecycle rules, assignment logic, and documentation standards.

Core capabilities

  • Case creation and classification
  • Lifecycle transitions
  • Assignments and escalations
  • Notes and protected notes
  • Evidence and attachments
  • Case linking and merging

Lifecycle enforcement

Cases must progress through required stages. Exceptions require administrative approval and are fully audited.

Audit logging

Every case action is logged, including views, edits, transitions, and assignments.

Reporting

Case metrics feed into dashboards, exports, and organizational risk analysis.
