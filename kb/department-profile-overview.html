Profile Overview

Department profiles provide a consolidated view of incidents, cases, readiness, and risk for a specific department or function, supporting leadership and HR reviews.

Profile components

  • Department details and hierarchy
  • Incident and case metrics
  • Severity and risk distribution
  • Training and readiness coverage
  • Open corrective actions

Comparative views

Departments can be compared across key metrics to identify outliers and best practices.

Access control

Department profiles are visible to authorized leaders, HR, and Security roles as defined by policy.
