Investigation Evidence Chain‑of‑Custody

How Relancy preserves the integrity and traceability of evidence throughout an investigation.

Overview

Chain‑of‑custody ensures that evidence remains authentic, unaltered, and fully traceable. Relancy enforces strict controls to maintain evidentiary integrity throughout the investigation lifecycle.

Chain‑of‑custody components

  • Uploader identity
  • Timestamp of upload
  • File hash for integrity verification
  • Access logs for views and downloads
  • Audit trail for edits or deletions

Access restrictions

Evidence is visible only to authorized HR and Security roles. Sensitive evidence may require elevated permissions.

Integrity safeguards

  • Immutable audit logs
  • Encrypted storage
  • Tenant‑isolated evidence containers

Compliance alignment

Chain‑of‑custody records support legal defensibility and regulatory compliance.
