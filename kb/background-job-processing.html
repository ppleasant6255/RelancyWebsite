Processing Overview

Background job processing enables Relancy to handle long‑running or asynchronous tasks without blocking user workflows. Jobs are queued, retried, and monitored for reliability.

Common background jobs

  • Notification delivery
  • Webhook dispatch
  • Data imports and exports
  • Readiness recalculation
  • Risk model updates

Reliability safeguards

  • Retry logic with exponential backoff
  • Dead‑letter queue for failed jobs
  • Tenant‑scoped isolation
  • Real‑time monitoring

Audit integrity

All job executions, failures, and retries are logged for operational and compliance review.
