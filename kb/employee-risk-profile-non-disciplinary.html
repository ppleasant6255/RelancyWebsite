Profile Overview

Employee Risk Profiles provide non‑disciplinary insights into readiness, training compliance, and behavioral patterns. Profiles support coaching, development, and early intervention—not punitive action.

Risk indicators

  • Readiness score trends
  • Training completion gaps
  • Corrective action follow‑through
  • Incident involvement patterns
  • Behavioral signals (non‑disciplinary)

Operational safeguards

Profiles exclude protected‑class data and cannot be used for disciplinary decisions without human review.

Audit integrity

Profile generation events and contributing factors are logged for transparency and compliance oversight.
