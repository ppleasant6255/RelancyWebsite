Case Templates and Standardization

How Relancy ensures consistent case creation and documentation across teams.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Case templates help organizations standardize how cases are created, documented, and managed. Templates ensure consistency across HR, Security, and operational teams while preserving audit integrity and lifecycle rules.

Template components

  • Default case category
  • Required fields
  • Suggested tags
  • Initial severity level
  • Checklist items or required steps
  • Default assignments or routing rules

Benefits of standardization

  • Consistent documentation across teams
  • Reduced onboarding time for new staff
  • Improved audit readiness
  • Clear expectations for case handling
  • Better reporting and trend analysis

Template governance

Templates are managed by authorized administrators. Changes are logged in the audit trail to preserve historical accuracy.

Private Deployment options

Private Deployment tenants may define advanced templates with custom logic or extended workflows.
