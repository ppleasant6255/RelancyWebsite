Taxonomy Overview

HR case taxonomy ensures consistent classification of employee relations issues. Categories and subcategories support reporting, routing, severity scoring, and compliance alignment.

Primary categories

  • Conduct issues
  • Harassment and discrimination
  • Policy violations
  • Attendance and performance
  • Compensation and benefits concerns
  • Interpersonal conflict

Subcategory examples

  • Inappropriate behavior
  • Protected‑class harassment
  • Timecard falsification
  • Chronic absenteeism
  • Retaliation concerns

Classification rules

Classification is based on incident details, reporter input, and organizational policy. Some categories trigger automatic severity or routing rules.

Audit integrity

All classification changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification to support defensibility.
