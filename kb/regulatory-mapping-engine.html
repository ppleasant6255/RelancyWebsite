Engine Overview

The Regulatory Mapping Engine links platform events and controls to regulatory requirements such as OSHA, EEOC, GDPR, and internal compliance frameworks.

Mapping capabilities

  • Category-to-regulation mapping
  • Control-to-requirement mapping
  • Automated compliance scoring
  • Regulatory gap analysis

Operational impact

Mapping supports audits, compliance reporting, and proactive risk management.

Audit integrity

Mapping rules and scoring events are logged for transparency and regulatory review.
