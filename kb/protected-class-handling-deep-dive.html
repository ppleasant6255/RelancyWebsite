Protected‑Class Handling (Deep Dive)

How Relancy safeguards protected‑class information across incidents, cases, reporting, and access control.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Protected‑class information requires heightened confidentiality and strict access boundaries. Relancy enforces specialized handling rules to ensure compliance with legal and organizational requirements.

Protected‑class indicators

  • Race and ethnicity
  • Gender identity
  • Sexual orientation
  • Religion
  • Disability status
  • Age‑related protections

Handling rules

  • Restricted visibility to authorized HR roles
  • Automatic redaction in reports
  • Special routing rules for sensitive incidents
  • Audit logging of all access attempts

Compliance alignment

Handling rules align with employment law, anti‑discrimination regulations, and internal policy.

Audit integrity

All interactions with protected‑class data are logged with timestamp, actor, and purpose.
