Sync Overview

Identity sync ensures that employee names, emails, roles, and identifiers remain accurate across Relancy and external identity systems. Syncs run on schedule or on‑demand.

Synced attributes

  • Legal and preferred names
  • Email and login identifiers
  • Job titles and roles
  • Manager relationships
  • Employment status

Operational safeguards

Identity conflicts trigger alerts and require HR or IT review. Protected‑class fields are never synced.

Audit integrity

Sync events include timestamp, source system, changed attributes, and validation results for defensibility.
