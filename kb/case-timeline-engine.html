Timeline Overview

The Case Timeline Engine automatically assembles a chronological record of all case events, including submissions, assignments, interviews, evidence uploads, corrective actions, and communications.

Timeline components

  • Case creation and routing events
  • Investigator actions
  • Interview and meeting logs
  • Evidence submissions
  • Corrective action lifecycle events
  • Final findings and closure

Operational safeguards

Timeline entries are immutable and automatically timestamped. Manual edits require justification and elevated permissions.

Audit integrity

Every timeline entry includes actor, timestamp, event type, and metadata for full defensibility and compliance alignment.
