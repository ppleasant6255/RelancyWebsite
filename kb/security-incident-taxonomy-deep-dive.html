Taxonomy Overview

Security incident taxonomy ensures consistent classification of safety, security, and environmental events. Categories support routing, escalation, and risk analysis.

Primary categories

  • Workplace violence
  • Theft or property damage
  • Unauthorized access
  • Safety hazards
  • Environmental incidents
  • Emergency events

Severity triggers

Certain categories automatically trigger high‑severity classification, such as violence, hazardous spills, or threats to life or property.

Routing rules

Security incidents route to designated Security roles, local leadership, or emergency response teams depending on category and severity.

Audit integrity

All classification and routing decisions are logged for compliance and operational review.
