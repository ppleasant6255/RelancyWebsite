Notification Template Governance

How Relancy governs email, SMS, and in‑app notification templates to ensure clarity, consistency, and compliance.

Governance Overview

Notification templates ensure consistent communication across incidents, cases, training, readiness, and corrective actions. Templates are governed to maintain clarity, compliance, and tenant branding.

Template types

  • Email notifications
  • SMS alerts
  • In‑app notifications
  • Escalation alerts
  • Training reminders

Governance rules

  • Templates must be clear, neutral, and actionable
  • Sensitive details must be excluded from SMS
  • Tenant branding may be applied to email templates
  • Templates must respect RBAC and confidentiality rules

Audit integrity

All template changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification for compliance and historical accuracy.
