External LMS Integration Guide

How Relancy integrates with external Learning Management Systems for training delivery and tracking.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy supports integration with external LMS platforms to synchronize training assignments and completions. This enables organizations to maintain a unified readiness record while using their preferred LMS tools.

Integration methods

  • Webhook‑based completion reporting
  • API‑based data pulls
  • Scheduled CSV imports

Required data fields

  • Employee identifier
  • Training module identifier
  • Completion date
  • Expiration date (if applicable)

Readiness integration

LMS completions feed directly into training lineage and readiness scoring, ensuring accurate compliance tracking.

Audit logging

All LMS‑related updates are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
