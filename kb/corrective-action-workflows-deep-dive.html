Workflow Overview

Corrective actions translate findings and incidents into concrete tasks with owners, due dates, and verification. Relancy ensures these actions are traceable, auditable, and linked to their originating events.

Core elements

  • Action description and category
  • Assigned owner and stakeholders
  • Due date and priority
  • Linked incident, case, or investigation
  • Verification and closure notes

Lifecycle

  • Proposed
  • Assigned
  • In progress
  • Completed
  • Verified

Escalation and reminders

Overdue actions trigger reminders and may escalate to managers or designated roles based on policy.

Audit integrity

All changes to corrective actions are logged, including ownership changes, due date updates, and verification.
