Engine Overview

The Case Quality Assurance Engine scores cases based on completeness, evidence quality, timeliness, and adherence to investigative standards.

Quality factors

  • Interview completeness
  • Evidence sufficiency
  • Timeline accuracy
  • Findings clarity
  • Corrective action alignment

Operational safeguards

QA scores cannot influence case outcomes. They are used for coaching, training, and process improvement only.

Audit integrity

QA scoring events and contributing factors are logged for transparency and defensibility.
