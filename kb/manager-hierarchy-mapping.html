Mapping Overview

Manager hierarchy mapping ensures accurate routing of incidents, approvals, and communications. Hierarchies are sourced from HRIS and validated during sync.

Hierarchy components

  • Direct manager relationships
  • Skip‑level visibility
  • Department leadership mapping
  • Location leadership mapping
  • Fallback routing for missing managers

Operational safeguards

Manager changes trigger re‑routing of open tasks and notifications. Conflicts require HR validation.

Audit integrity

Hierarchy updates are logged with timestamp, actor, and changed relationships for compliance review.
