Findings & Determination Framework

How Relancy structures findings, determinations, and case outcomes using standardized, auditable frameworks.

Back to Knowledge Base

Framework Overview

Findings summarize the investigator’s conclusions based on evidence, interviews, and policy alignment. Determinations classify the case outcome using standardized categories.

Determination categories

  • Substantiated
  • Unsubstantiated
  • Inconclusive
  • Policy violation confirmed
  • No policy violation

Operational safeguards

Findings require justification and cannot be altered after approval without elevated permissions and audit review.

Audit integrity

Findings and determinations are logged with timestamp, actor, evidence references, and policy citations.
