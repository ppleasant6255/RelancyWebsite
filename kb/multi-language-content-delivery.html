Delivery Overview

Multi‑language delivery ensures that employees receive content in their preferred language. Translations follow controlled workflows to maintain accuracy and compliance.

Translation components

  • Source content versioning
  • Human or certified translation
  • Automated fallback translation (optional)
  • Language preference detection
  • Localized delivery

Operational safeguards

Translations cannot alter policy meaning. Sensitive content requires certified translation only.

Audit integrity

Translation events include timestamp, translator identity, version metadata, and language mapping for compliance alignment.
