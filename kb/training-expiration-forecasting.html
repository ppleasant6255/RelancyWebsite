Training Expiration Forecasting

How Relancy predicts upcoming training expirations to support proactive readiness management.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Training expiration forecasting helps organizations anticipate readiness gaps before they occur. Relancy analyzes training lineage to identify upcoming expirations and renewal requirements.

Forecasting inputs

  • Training completion dates
  • Expiration cycles
  • Role‑based requirements
  • Department‑level requirements

Forecasting outputs

  • Upcoming expirations
  • Overdue items
  • Renewal recommendations
  • Readiness impact projections

Notifications

Employees, managers, and training coordinators receive alerts when expirations approach.

Reporting

Forecasting data appears in readiness dashboards and exports.
