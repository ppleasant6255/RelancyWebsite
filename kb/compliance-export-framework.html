Framework Overview

The Compliance Export Framework provides structured, redacted, and audit-ready exports for regulatory and legal processes. Exports follow strict privacy and security rules.

Export types

  • Incident and case summaries
  • Audit logs
  • Training and readiness records
  • Corrective action reports
  • Risk and trend analysis

Redaction rules

Sensitive fields are removed or anonymized based on tenant policy and regulatory requirements.

Audit integrity

All compliance exports are logged with timestamp, actor, and export parameters for defensibility.
