Case Linking & Merging Rules

How Relancy links related cases and merges duplicates while preserving audit integrity.

Linking Overview

Relancy supports linking related cases to provide full context and traceability. Linking is reversible, auditable, and governed by RBAC.

Merging rules

  • Only authorized users may merge cases
  • All notes, evidence, and history are preserved
  • The primary case becomes the canonical record
  • Secondary cases are archived but remain auditable

Audit logging

Linking and merging events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification to maintain historical accuracy.
