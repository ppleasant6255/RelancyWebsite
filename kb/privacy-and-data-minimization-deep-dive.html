Privacy Overview

Relancy follows strict privacy principles, collecting only the data necessary for operational workflows and compliance. Data minimization reduces risk and supports regulatory alignment.

Minimization principles

  • Collect only required fields
  • Limit visibility based on RBAC
  • Redact sensitive fields in exports
  • Restrict offline caching of sensitive data
  • Apply retention rules to remove stale data

Operational impact

Minimization ensures that employees, managers, and administrators only see the information necessary for their responsibilities.

Compliance alignment

Minimization supports privacy regulations and internal governance standards.

Audit logging

Access to sensitive fields is logged to maintain transparency and accountability.
