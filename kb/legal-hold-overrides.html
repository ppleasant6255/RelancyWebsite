Legal Hold Overrides

How Relancy governs overrides to legal hold restrictions while preserving audit integrity and compliance.

Back to Knowledge Base

Override Overview

Legal hold overrides allow authorized legal or compliance personnel to modify or remove legal holds under exceptional circumstances. Overrides are tightly controlled and fully auditable.

Valid override scenarios

  • Legal matter resolved
  • Incorrect legal hold assignment
  • Administrative correction
  • Regulatory directive

Authorization

Only designated legal or compliance administrators may override legal holds. Overrides require justification and multi‑factor approval.

Audit logging

All overrides are logged with timestamp, actor, justification, and affected records. Overrides cannot be hidden or deleted.
