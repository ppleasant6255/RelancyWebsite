Time Zone Handling

How Relancy manages timestamps, scheduling, and cross‑region consistency.

Back to Knowledge Base

Time Zone Overview

Relancy stores all timestamps in UTC and converts them to the user’s local time zone at runtime. This ensures consistency across global teams and prevents ambiguity in audit logs.

Conversion rules

  • All stored timestamps use UTC
  • UI displays convert to user’s local time
  • Exports may include both UTC and local time
  • Audit logs always display UTC for defensibility

Scheduling behavior

Notifications, escalations, and expiration cycles use UTC internally to avoid drift across regions.

Cross‑region collaboration

Users in different time zones see timestamps in their local format without affecting underlying data.
