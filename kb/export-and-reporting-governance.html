Governance Overview

Export and reporting governance ensures that sensitive data is protected during extraction and that reports follow compliance, privacy, and redaction rules.

Governance controls

  • Role-based export permissions
  • Automatic redaction of protected data
  • Export format validation
  • Report versioning
  • Legal hold enforcement

Operational safeguards

Sensitive exports require elevated approval. Exported files are watermarked and logged for traceability.

Audit integrity

Export events include timestamp, actor, dataset, redaction status, and delivery method for defensibility.
