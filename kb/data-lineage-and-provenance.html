Lineage Overview

Data lineage provides visibility into where data originated, how it was transformed, and where it was exported. Provenance supports compliance, audits, and operational transparency.

Tracked lineage events

  • Data creation and updates
  • Cross‑module transformations
  • Export mappings
  • Redaction and anonymization steps
  • Delivery to external systems

Operational impact

Lineage ensures that organizations can trace data back to its source during audits or investigations.

Audit integrity

Lineage logs are immutable and retained according to tenant retention policy.
