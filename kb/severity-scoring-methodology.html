Severity Model Overview

Severity scoring ensures consistent classification of incidents and cases. Relancy evaluates multiple factors to assign severity automatically, with manual overrides allowed for authorized users.

Severity factors

  • Type of incident or case
  • Protected‑class involvement
  • Injury or safety risk
  • Repeat behavior
  • Location risk profile
  • Policy‑defined triggers

Override rules

Authorized users may adjust severity when appropriate. Overrides require justification and are fully audited.

Operational impact

Severity influences routing, escalation, reporting, and risk scoring.

Audit logging

All severity assignments and overrides are logged with timestamp and contributing factors.
