Rules Overview

Broadcast messaging allows leadership and HR/Security teams to send organization‑wide communications. Messages follow strict approval and targeting rules.

Broadcast controls

  • Role‑based sender permissions
  • Mandatory approval for sensitive topics
  • Location and department targeting
  • Multi‑language delivery
  • Delivery status tracking

Operational safeguards

Broadcasts cannot include protected‑class data or confidential case details. Sensitive broadcasts require dual approval.

Audit integrity

Broadcast events include timestamp, sender, recipients, approval chain, and delivery results for compliance review.
