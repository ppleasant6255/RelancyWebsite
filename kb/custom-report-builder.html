Builder Overview

The Custom Report Builder allows administrators to create tailored reports using configurable filters, field selections, and export formats. Reports support compliance, audits, and leadership reviews.

Report components

  • Entity selection (incidents, cases, training, etc.)
  • Filter configuration
  • Field selection
  • Sorting and grouping
  • Export options

Operational impact

Custom reports provide flexibility for organizations with unique reporting requirements or regulatory needs.

Audit integrity

Report definitions and export events are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
