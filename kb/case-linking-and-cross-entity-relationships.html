Case Linking and Cross-Entity Relationships

How Relancy connects incidents, investigations, training, and readiness.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy unifies operational data by linking related entities such as cases, incidents, investigations, training assignments, and readiness records. This creates a complete operational narrative and ensures that nothing is overlooked.

Case as the central entity

The case serves as the anchor for all related activity. A case may include:

  • One or more incidents
  • An investigation
  • Training assignments
  • Readiness evaluations
  • Notes and attachments

Incident relationships

Multiple incidents can be linked to a single case when they share context, actors, or operational impact.

Investigation relationships

Investigations are linked to cases to provide a structured, auditable record of findings and recommendations.

Training and readiness relationships

Training assignments and readiness evaluations are linked to cases when corrective action or preparedness is part of the operational response.

Audit and traceability

All relationships are recorded in the audit log, ensuring a complete historical record of how entities relate to one another.
