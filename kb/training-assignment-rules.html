Assignment Overview

Training assignments are determined by employee role, department, location, risk profile, and compliance requirements. Assignments are automated and auditable.

Assignment factors

  • Job role and responsibilities
  • Location‑specific regulations
  • Departmental risk profile
  • Corrective action requirements
  • Leadership or HR directives

Operational safeguards

Assignments cannot be removed without justification. High‑risk employees receive mandatory training overrides.

Audit integrity

Assignment events include timestamp, actor, justification, and module metadata for defensibility.
