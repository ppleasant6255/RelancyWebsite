Webhook Retry and Delivery Guarantees

How Relancy ensures reliable delivery of webhook events to external systems.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Webhooks provide real‑time notifications to external systems. Relancy ensures reliable delivery through retry logic, delivery guarantees, and detailed logging.

Delivery guarantees

  • At‑least‑once delivery
  • Ordered delivery per entity
  • Secure HTTPS transmission

Retry logic

Failed deliveries are retried using exponential backoff. Persistent failures are logged and may trigger alerts.

Failure handling

  • Automatic retry queueing
  • Dead‑letter logging for unresolved failures
  • Administrative review tools

Audit logging

All webhook attempts, successes, and failures are logged for operational review.
