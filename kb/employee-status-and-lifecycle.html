Employee Status and Lifecycle

How Relancy manages employee states from onboarding to separation.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Employee status determines visibility, readiness tracking, and operational involvement. Relancy maintains a structured lifecycle to ensure accurate reporting and compliance across HR and Security workflows.

Status types

  • Active: Fully included in readiness, training, and case workflows.
  • Leave: Temporarily excluded from certain workflows depending on policy.
  • Inactive: No longer active but retained for historical reporting.
  • Terminated: Removed from operational workflows but preserved in audit history.

Lifecycle transitions

Common transitions include:

  • Onboarding → Active
  • Active → Leave
  • Leave → Active
  • Active → Terminated

All transitions are logged in the audit trail with timestamp, actor, and reason.

Impact on readiness

Employee status affects readiness scoring. For example, terminated or inactive employees are excluded from readiness calculations.

Data retention

Even after termination, employee records remain available for historical reporting and compliance purposes.
