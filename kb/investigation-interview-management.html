Investigation Interview Management

How Relancy structures and documents interviews during investigations.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Interviews are a core component of investigations. Relancy provides structured tools to document interview details, capture statements, and maintain audit integrity.

Interview components

  • Interviewee identity
  • Date and time
  • Interviewer(s)
  • Summary of statements
  • Attachments or transcripts
  • Follow‑up actions

Best practices

  • Use neutral, factual language
  • Record direct quotes when relevant
  • Document clarifying questions
  • Capture non‑verbal cues only when operationally relevant

Access control

Interview records are restricted to authorized HR and Security roles. Sensitive interviews may require elevated permissions.

Audit logging

All interview entries, edits, and views are logged in the audit trail.
