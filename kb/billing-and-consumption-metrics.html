Metrics Overview

Billing and consumption metrics track tenant usage of platform features to support accurate invoicing and subscription compliance.

Tracked consumption

  • Notification volume
  • Storage usage
  • API call volume
  • Background job throughput
  • Training module consumption

Operational safeguards

Consumption is tracked in real time and validated to prevent billing anomalies or overages.

Audit integrity

Billing events and consumption logs are retained for financial audits and tenant review.
