Module Overview

The Readiness module provides a unified view of employee preparedness across roles, departments, and locations. It aggregates training, certifications, and compliance requirements into a single readiness score.

Readiness components

  • Training completions
  • Certification status
  • Role‑based requirements
  • Department‑level requirements
  • Expiration cycles

Scoring model

Readiness scores are calculated using a weighted model that reflects organizational priorities and compliance requirements.

Dashboards

Readiness dashboards highlight gaps, upcoming expirations, and department‑level trends.

Audit integrity

All readiness changes are logged and traceable to underlying training or certification events.
