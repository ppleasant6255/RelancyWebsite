Governance Overview

Training Program Governance ensures that training content, assignments, and assessments remain compliant, accurate, and aligned with organizational policies.

Governance components

  • Content versioning
  • Compliance alignment
  • Assignment rule validation
  • Assessment quality review
  • Leadership oversight

Operational safeguards

Training updates require justification and cannot retroactively alter completed records without audit review.

Audit integrity

All governance actions are logged with timestamp, actor, and version metadata for defensibility and compliance alignment.
