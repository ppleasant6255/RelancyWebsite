Retention Overview

Data retention policies ensure that records are preserved for required periods and purged when no longer needed. Relancy enforces retention rules to support compliance and reduce risk.

Retention categories

  • Incidents
  • Cases and investigations
  • Training and readiness records
  • Corrective actions
  • Audit logs

Purge rules

  • Purge occurs only after retention period expires
  • Legal hold overrides purge
  • Purge events are logged
  • Purge is irreversible

Compliance alignment

Retention schedules align with regulatory requirements and tenant policy. Administrators may configure retention periods.
