Case Aging and SLA Tracking

How Relancy monitors case duration and enforces service-level expectations.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Case aging and SLA tracking help organizations ensure timely response to incidents and investigations. Relancy provides built-in tools to monitor case duration, identify delays, and support compliance with internal or regulatory timelines.

Case aging metrics

  • Time since case creation
  • Time in current lifecycle stage
  • Total time to resolution
  • Overdue status based on SLA

SLA configuration

Administrators can define SLAs based on:

  • Case category
  • Severity level
  • Department or location

Notifications and escalations

Relancy automatically notifies owners when cases approach or exceed SLA thresholds. Escalations may be triggered for critical delays.

Reporting

SLA performance is included in dashboards and exports, helping organizations identify bottlenecks and improve operational efficiency.

Audit integrity

All SLA-related events are logged in the audit trail, ensuring transparency and accountability.
