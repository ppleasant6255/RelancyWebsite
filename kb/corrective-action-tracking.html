Corrective Action Tracking

How Relancy manages follow-up actions resulting from cases and investigations.

Overview

Corrective actions help organizations address findings from cases, incidents, and investigations. Relancy provides structured tools to assign, track, and verify corrective actions across teams.

Corrective action types

  • Training assignments
  • Policy acknowledgments
  • Process changes
  • Environmental or equipment fixes
  • Coaching or performance improvement steps

Assignment and ownership

Corrective actions are assigned to individuals or teams with clear due dates and expectations. Ownership is visible in case and investigation views.

Tracking and verification

Relancy tracks progress and completion. Verification steps may include:

  • Manager approval
  • Training completion
  • Documented evidence of remediation

Audit and reporting

All corrective actions are logged in the audit trail and included in readiness and compliance reporting.
