Employee Offboarding Workflow

How Relancy manages employee offboarding to maintain data integrity and operational continuity.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Offboarding requires careful handling of access, training, and case visibility. Relancy enforces structured workflows to ensure offboarding is secure, compliant, and fully auditable.

Offboarding steps

  • Update employee status to “Terminated”
  • Reassign open cases
  • Revoke access permissions
  • Preserve historical records
  • Trigger optional exit workflows

Data retention

Employee records remain available for historical reporting and compliance, even after termination.

Audit logging

All offboarding actions are logged for compliance and operational review.

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may integrate offboarding with HRIS or identity management systems.
