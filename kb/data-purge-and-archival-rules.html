How Relancy securely purges or archives data based on retention policies and tenant configuration.

Purge Overview

Data purging removes expired records from the platform using secure, irreversible deletion. Archival preserves long-term records in a compressed, read-only format.

Purge rules

  • Triggered by retention expiration
  • Validated for dependencies
  • Performed using secure deletion
  • Logged for compliance

Archival rules

  • Read-only storage
  • Encrypted at rest
  • Tenant-scoped isolation
  • Retrievable by administrators

Audit integrity

All purge and archival events are logged with timestamp, actor, and affected datasets.
