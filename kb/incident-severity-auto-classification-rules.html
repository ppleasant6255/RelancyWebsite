Auto‑Classification Overview

Incident severity is automatically assigned using structured rules that evaluate category, risk factors, and organizational policy. Auto‑classification ensures consistent triage and routing.

Severity triggers

  • Violence or threat indicators
  • Safety hazards or injuries
  • Environmental or emergency events
  • Unauthorized access or security breaches
  • Protected‑class involvement

Override rules

Authorized HR or Security roles may override severity when necessary. Overrides require justification and are fully audited.

Audit integrity

All severity assignments and overrides are logged with timestamp, actor, and contributing factors.
