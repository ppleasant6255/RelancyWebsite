Employee Profile Structure

How Relancy organizes and manages employee information.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Employee profiles serve as the foundation for training, readiness, case management, and operational reporting. Relancy maintains a structured, privacy-conscious profile model that supports HR and Security workflows without exposing unnecessary information.

Core profile fields

Every employee profile includes essential operational fields such as:

  • Name
  • Employee ID
  • Department
  • Location
  • Role or job title
  • Manager or supervisor
  • Status (active, inactive, terminated)

Training and readiness fields

Profiles include training lineage and readiness indicators, enabling accurate readiness scoring and compliance reporting.

Protected-class fields

Protected-class data is stored separately, encrypted, and access-restricted. It is never displayed in general profile views unless explicitly required by policy.

Custom fields

Tenants may add custom fields to employee profiles to capture organization-specific information. Custom fields do not affect platform behavior.

Audit and history

All changes to employee profiles are logged in the audit trail, including updates to role, department, or status.
