Engine Overview

The Corrective Action Engine orchestrates the full lifecycle of corrective actions—from creation to verification. Actions are structured, auditable, and aligned with regulatory and organizational requirements.

Action types

  • Training assignments
  • Policy acknowledgments
  • Coaching sessions
  • Environmental or equipment fixes
  • Process or workflow changes

Lifecycle stages

  • Creation and justification
  • Assignment and notification
  • Progress tracking
  • Completion validation
  • Final verification

Operational safeguards

Actions cannot be closed without verification. High‑risk actions require multi‑level approval and evidence.

Audit integrity

Every lifecycle stage is logged with timestamp, actor, and evidence attachments for defensibility.
