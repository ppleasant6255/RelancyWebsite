Overview

Training delivery must meet internal policy and external regulatory standards. Relancy enforces compliance through structured delivery methods, audit logging, and readiness integration.

Compliance requirements

  • Accurate tracking of completions
  • Version control for training content
  • Documented delivery method
  • Expiration and renewal cycles
  • Role‑based assignment rules

Supported delivery methods

  • Online modules
  • Instructor‑led sessions
  • Video‑based training
  • Policy acknowledgments

Audit and reporting

All training activity is logged and available in readiness dashboards and compliance reports.
