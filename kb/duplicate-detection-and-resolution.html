Duplicate Overview

Duplicate detection ensures data accuracy and prevents fragmented case histories. Relancy uses structured rules to identify potential duplicates and guide resolution.

Detection rules

  • Matching employee identifiers
  • Similar incident descriptions
  • Overlapping timestamps
  • Duplicate evidence uploads
  • Repeated submissions from the same reporter

Resolution options

  • Merge duplicates
  • Link related records
  • Mark as non‑duplicate

Audit integrity

All duplicate detection and resolution actions are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
