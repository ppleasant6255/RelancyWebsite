Engine Overview

The Tagging & Classification Engine applies structured tags and categories to records based on tenant rules, system logic, and user input. Classification supports reporting, routing, and risk modeling.

Classification inputs

  • Category and subcategory
  • Severity level
  • Location and department
  • Employee role
  • Behavioral patterns

Operational safeguards

Classification changes require justification and are fully audited. System‑generated classifications are transparent and explainable.

Audit integrity

All classification events—including overrides—are logged with timestamp, actor, and contributing factors.
