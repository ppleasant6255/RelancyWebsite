Timeline Overview

The employee timeline consolidates all relevant events for an employee, including training completions, readiness changes, incidents, cases, and corrective actions. Visibility is governed by RBAC.

Timeline components

  • Training assignments and completions
  • Readiness score changes
  • Incidents involving the employee
  • Cases involving the employee
  • Corrective actions assigned to the employee
  • Role or department changes

Privacy boundaries

Sensitive HR or Security information is visible only to authorized roles. Employees may see only their own training and readiness events.

Audit integrity

All timeline entries are logged and immutable, supporting defensibility and compliance alignment.
