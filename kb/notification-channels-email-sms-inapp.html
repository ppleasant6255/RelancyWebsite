Channel Overview

Relancy supports three primary notification channels: email, SMS, and in‑app notifications. Each channel serves different operational needs and respects user preferences, RBAC boundaries, and tenant policy.

Email notifications

  • Used for assignments, escalations, and overdue reminders
  • Includes deep links to relevant entities
  • Supports tenant branding
  • Follows user notification preferences

SMS notifications

SMS is used for urgent or time‑sensitive alerts such as severe incidents or critical escalations. SMS delivery requires explicit user opt‑in and administrator approval.

In‑app notifications

  • Displayed in the notification center
  • Route users
