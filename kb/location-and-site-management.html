Location and Site Management

How Relancy organizes physical locations for reporting and operational workflows.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Locations and sites help organizations track where incidents occur, where employees work, and how operational patterns vary across facilities. Relancy provides structured tools for managing location data.

Location types

  • Corporate offices
  • Warehouses
  • Retail stores
  • Manufacturing sites
  • Remote or field locations

Location attributes

Each location may include:

  • Address
  • Region or division
  • Site manager
  • Operating hours
  • Risk profile

Operational benefits

  • Location‑based reporting
  • Pattern detection across sites
  • Targeted training and readiness
  • Incident clustering analysis

Audit and updates

Changes to location data are logged in the audit trail to maintain historical accuracy.
