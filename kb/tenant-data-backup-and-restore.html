Tenant Data Backup and Restore

How Relancy safeguards tenant data through automated backups and controlled restore workflows.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy performs automated backups to protect tenant data against loss, corruption, or catastrophic failure. Restore operations are tightly controlled to maintain audit integrity and tenant isolation.

Backup schedule

  • Daily full backups
  • Frequent incremental backups
  • Encrypted storage of all backup artifacts

Restore workflow

Restore operations are performed only by authorized platform administrators. Tenants may request restores for:

  • Accidental data deletion
  • Corrupted data
  • Disaster recovery scenarios

Audit logging

All restore operations are logged with timestamp, actor, and scope of restored data.

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may define custom backup schedules or integrate external backup systems.
