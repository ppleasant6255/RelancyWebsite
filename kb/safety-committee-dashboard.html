Dashboard Overview

The Safety Committee Dashboard provides anonymized, aggregated insights into incidents, hazards, and corrective actions. It supports proactive safety planning and compliance with workplace safety standards.

Key features

  • Incident trend analysis
  • Hazard identification patterns
  • Corrective action tracking
  • Location‑level risk indicators
  • Root cause summaries

Privacy safeguards

All data is de‑identified to protect employee privacy. Sensitive HR or Security cases are excluded.

Operational integration

Safety committee insights feed into corrective action planning, training updates, and leadership reporting.
