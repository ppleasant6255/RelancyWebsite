Case Escalation Policies

How Relancy enforces structured escalation rules for high‑risk or time‑sensitive cases.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Escalation policies ensure that high‑severity or overdue cases receive timely attention from the appropriate personnel. Relancy automates escalation triggers based on severity, SLA thresholds, and organizational policy.

Escalation triggers

  • Critical severity cases
  • Missed SLA deadlines
  • Repeat incidents involving the same individual
  • Cases with protected‑class implications
  • Leadership‑defined escalation rules

Escalation actions

  • Reassignment to senior staff
  • Leadership notification
  • Additional review requirements
  • Mandatory documentation updates

Audit logging

All escalations are logged with timestamp, actor, and reason to ensure transparency and defensibility.

Private Deployment options

Private Deployment tenants may define custom escalation logic or integrate external escalation systems.
