Risk Model Overview

Relancy uses a weighted scoring model to evaluate risk across individuals, locations, and categories. Scores help organizations identify patterns and prioritize interventions.

Risk factors

  • Incident frequency
  • Case severity distribution
  • Repeat behavior indicators
  • Location‑based hazards
  • Training and readiness gaps

Scoring behavior

Scores are recalculated automatically as new data arrives. High‑risk entities may trigger alerts or escalations.

Reporting

Risk scores appear in dashboards, exports, and leadership reports.

Audit integrity

All scoring events are logged with timestamp and contributing factors.
