Onboarding Overview

Onboarding workflows ensure that new employees are properly configured in Relancy, assigned required training, and initialized with accurate readiness and role information. Onboarding supports compliance and operational readiness.

Onboarding tasks

  • Role and department assignment
  • Location assignment
  • Training and certification initialization
  • Readiness score initialization
  • Policy acknowledgment distribution

Operational impact

Proper onboarding ensures accurate routing, visibility, and readiness scoring from day one.

Audit integrity

All onboarding events are logged for compliance and historical traceability.
