Overview

Compliance audits require accurate documentation, consistent workflows, and complete historical records. Relancy provides structured tools and reporting to support audit readiness across HR, Security, and leadership.

Audit preparation steps

  • Review case lifecycle completion
  • Verify training compliance and readiness scores
  • Confirm evidence integrity and chain‑of‑custody
  • Export required reports
  • Validate access permissions and RBAC assignments

Recommended reports

  • Case summary report
  • Incident trend analysis
  • Training compliance report
  • Corrective action status
  • Access review logs

Audit logging

All operational activity is logged automatically, ensuring a complete and defensible audit trail.

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may integrate Relancy data with external audit systems or compliance platforms.
