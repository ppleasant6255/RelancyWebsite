Case Notes and Communication Guidelines

How to document case activity clearly, professionally, and defensibly.

Overview

Case notes are a critical part of the operational record. Relancy provides structured note-taking tools to ensure that documentation is clear, consistent, and legally defensible.

Best practices for case notes

  • Record facts, not assumptions.
  • Use neutral, professional language.
  • Include dates, times, and participants.
  • Document actions taken and next steps.
  • Avoid personal opinions or speculation.

Note visibility

Notes are visible only to authorized users based on RBAC. Sensitive notes may be restricted further depending on case type.

Audit logging

All notes are timestamped and attributed to the author. Edits and deletions are logged to preserve historical accuracy.

Communication boundaries

Relancy is not a chat platform. Notes should be used for documentation, not informal conversation.
