Case Attachments Retention Rules

How Relancy manages the lifecycle of documents, media, and evidence attached to cases.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Attachments and evidence follow strict retention rules to support compliance, audit integrity, and operational continuity. Relancy ensures that attachments remain available as long as required and are removed safely when appropriate.

Retention periods

  • Case attachments: retained for the life of the case
  • Investigation evidence: retained indefinitely
  • System logs: retained per platform policy

Deletion rules

Attachments may be deleted only by authorized users and only when allowed by policy. Deletions are logged and do not remove historical audit entries.

Compliance considerations

Organizations may define additional retention requirements based on legal or regulatory obligations.

Private Deployment options

Private Deployment tenants may define custom retention schedules or integrate external archival systems.
