Engine Overview

The Safety Communications Engine distributes safety alerts, policy updates, and training reminders to employees using targeted, role‑aware messaging channels.

Communication types

  • Safety alerts
  • Policy updates
  • Training reminders
  • Corrective action notifications
  • Leadership broadcasts

Operational safeguards

Messages respect RBAC, location restrictions, and language preferences. Sensitive messages require elevated approval.

Audit integrity

All communications are logged with timestamp, sender, recipients, and delivery status for compliance review.
