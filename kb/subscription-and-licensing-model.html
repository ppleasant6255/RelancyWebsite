Model Overview

Relancy uses a subscription‑based licensing model with tiered features, user entitlements, and usage limits. Licensing ensures predictable billing and scalable tenant growth.

Subscription tiers

  • Standard – core incident and case management
  • Professional – advanced workflows and reporting
  • Enterprise – full platform access with regulatory modules

Licensing rules

  • Per‑user or per‑tenant licensing
  • Optional add‑ons (training, readiness, regulatory modules)
  • Usage‑based billing for high‑volume features

Audit integrity

Subscription changes and license assignments are logged for billing accuracy and compliance review.
