Profile Overview

Location profiles aggregate incidents, cases, readiness, and risk indicators for a specific site or facility. They support local leadership, safety teams, and corporate oversight.

Profile components

  • Location details and hierarchy
  • Incident and case volume
  • Severity and risk trends
  • Readiness and training coverage
  • Open corrective actions

Dashboards

Location dashboards highlight hotspots, recurring issues, and readiness gaps.

Access control

Access to location profiles is governed by role, region, and organizational policy.
