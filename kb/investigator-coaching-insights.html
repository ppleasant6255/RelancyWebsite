Insights Overview

Coaching insights help investigators improve their investigative skills, documentation quality, and adherence to best practices. Insights are advisory and non‑disciplinary.

Insight categories

  • Timeliness improvements
  • Evidence completeness
  • Interview quality
  • Findings clarity
  • Documentation accuracy

Operational safeguards

Coaching insights exclude protected‑class data and cannot be used for punitive decisions without human review.

Audit integrity

Insight generation events are logged with timestamp, actor, and contributing factors for transparency.
