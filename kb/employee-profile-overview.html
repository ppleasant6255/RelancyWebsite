Profile Overview

Employee profiles consolidate key information, including role, department, location, readiness, and related cases or incidents. Profiles respect strict privacy and RBAC boundaries.

Profile components

  • Basic details (name, role, department, location)
  • Manager and reporting line
  • Training and readiness status
  • Relevant incidents and cases (subject to permissions)
  • Corrective actions involving the employee

Timeline view

A chronological timeline shows key events such as training completions, incidents, and case milestones.

Privacy boundaries

Sensitive HR or Security information is only visible to authorized roles, even when viewing an employee profile.
