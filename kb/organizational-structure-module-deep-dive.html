Module Overview

The Organizational Structure module defines the hierarchy of departments, locations, and reporting lines. It provides the backbone for visibility rules, routing logic, readiness requirements, and leadership dashboards.

Core capabilities

  • Department and location management
  • Manager hierarchy modeling
  • Role and team assignments
  • Cross‑functional reporting structures
  • Impact analysis for organizational changes

Operational integration

  • Case visibility rules
  • Incident routing
  • Training and readiness requirements
  • Manager dashboards
  • Escalation paths

Audit logging

All structural changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification to preserve historical accuracy.
