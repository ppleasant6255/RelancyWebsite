Trend Analysis Overview

Relancy analyzes historical incidents, cases, training completions, and readiness scores to identify trends and forecast future risks. Forecasting supports proactive planning and resource allocation.

Trend types

  • Incident frequency trends
  • Severity distribution shifts
  • Training compliance patterns
  • Readiness score changes
  • Corrective action performance trends

Forecasting models

Forecasting uses weighted historical data to predict future risk, readiness gaps, and compliance needs. Models are transparent and auditable.

Operational impact

Forecasts support leadership planning, staffing decisions, and targeted training initiatives.

Audit integrity

Forecasting inputs and outputs are logged for transparency and defensibility.
