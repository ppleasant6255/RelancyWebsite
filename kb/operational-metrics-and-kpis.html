Overview

Relancy provides a suite of operational metrics and KPIs to help organizations monitor performance, identify trends, and improve decision-making. These metrics are available through dashboards, reports, and exports.

Key performance indicators

  • Incident volume: Total incidents over time.
  • Case resolution time: Average time to close cases.
  • Investigation duration: Time spent in each lifecycle stage.
  • Training completion rate: Percentage of required training completed.
  • Readiness score: Overall preparedness of teams.
  • Corrective action completion: Percentage of actions completed on time.

Trend analysis

Relancy highlights patterns across departments, locations, and time periods to help leaders identify emerging risks and operational gaps.

Reporting and exports

All KPIs can be exported for offline analysis or integrated into BI tools via the reporting API.

Audit and transparency

KPI calculations are based on immutable audit and lifecycle data, ensuring accuracy and defensibility.
