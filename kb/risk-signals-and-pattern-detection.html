Risk Signals and Pattern Detection

How Relancy identifies emerging risks across incidents, cases, and training.

Overview

Relancy analyzes operational data to identify risk signals and emerging patterns. These insights help organizations detect issues early, respond proactively, and strengthen their safety and compliance posture.

Types of risk signals

  • Behavioral patterns: Repeat incidents involving the same individual or group.
  • Environmental patterns: Issues concentrated in a location or department.
  • Training gaps: Readiness deficiencies correlated with incidents.
  • Escalation frequency: Cases requiring repeated intervention.
  • Policy deviations: Actions outside expected lifecycle behavior.

Pattern detection

Relancy correlates data across cases, incidents, investigations, and training lineage to surface meaningful patterns. These patterns are displayed in dashboards and can trigger notifications.

Operational impact

Risk signals support:

  • Early intervention
  • Policy refinement
  • Targeted training
  • Resource allocation
  • Leadership reporting

Audit and transparency

All risk signals and pattern detections are logged for audit and review, ensuring transparency and accountability.
