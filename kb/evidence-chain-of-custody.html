Chain‑of‑Custody Overview

Relancy maintains a complete chain‑of‑custody for all evidence, documenting every upload, access, modification, and transfer event to ensure legal defensibility.

Tracked events

  • Evidence upload and source metadata
  • Access logs with actor identity
  • Versioning and modification history
  • Transfer or reassignment events
  • Deletion or archival actions

Operational safeguards

Evidence is encrypted, access‑controlled, and isolated per tenant. Sensitive evidence requires elevated permissions.

Audit integrity

Chain‑of‑custody logs are immutable and retained according to tenant retention and legal hold policies.
