Organizational Change Impact Analysis

How Relancy evaluates the impact of organizational changes on readiness, reporting, and case workflows.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Organizational changes—such as restructuring, mergers, or department realignment—affect reporting, readiness, and case visibility. Relancy provides tools to analyze and manage these impacts.

Impacted areas

  • Manager hierarchies
  • Department‑level reporting
  • Training requirements
  • Case visibility and routing
  • Readiness scoring

Change management workflow

  • Review proposed changes
  • Assess readiness and training impact
  • Update organizational structure
  • Validate reporting accuracy
  • Communicate changes to stakeholders

Audit logging

All organizational changes are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may integrate organizational changes with HRIS or identity systems.
