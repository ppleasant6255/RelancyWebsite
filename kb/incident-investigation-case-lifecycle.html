Incident, Investigation, and Case Lifecycle

How Relancy structures operational response from first signal to final resolution.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy provides a structured lifecycle for incidents, investigations, and cases. This ensures that operational response is consistent, auditable, and aligned with organizational policy.

Incident lifecycle

Incidents typically move through:

  • Reported
  • Validated
  • Assigned
  • In Progress
  • Resolved
  • Closed

Each transition is recorded in the audit log with full context.

Investigation lifecycle

Investigations follow a deeper, more structured path:

  • Opened
  • Fact Gathering
  • Analysis
  • Findings
  • Recommendations
  • Closed

The platform enforces required steps to ensure thoroughness and defensibility.

Case lifecycle

Cases unify incidents, investigations, training, and readiness into a single operational narrative. The case lifecycle ensures that all related activity is captured and resolved before closure.

  • Opened
  • Under Review
  • Action Required
  • Monitoring
  • Resolved
  • Closed

Why lifecycle enforcement matters

  • Ensures consistent handling of sensitive events
  • Supports legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevents premature closure
  • Maintains a defensible operational record

Integrations and lifecycle events

Tenants can subscribe to lifecycle events via webhooks to trigger external workflows, notifications, or automations. These integrations react to lifecycle changes but cannot alter them.
