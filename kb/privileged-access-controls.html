Controls Overview

Privileged access controls ensure that sensitive actions—such as case reassignment, redaction, and legal review— are restricted to authorized roles with elevated permissions.

Privileged actions

  • Viewing confidential notes
  • Accessing protected‑class data
  • Performing redactions
  • Reopening cases
  • Modifying routing rules

Operational safeguards

Privileged roles require MFA, shorter session timeouts, and enhanced monitoring. Unauthorized attempts trigger alerts.

Audit integrity

All privileged actions are logged with timestamp, actor, justification, and affected records for defensibility.
