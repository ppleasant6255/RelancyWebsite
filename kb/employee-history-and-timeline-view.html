Overview

The employee timeline provides a chronological view of all operational activity involving an employee. This includes incidents, cases, investigations, training lineage, readiness changes, and corrective actions.

Timeline components

  • Incident reports
  • Case assignments and transitions
  • Investigation milestones
  • Training completions and expirations
  • Readiness status changes
  • Corrective actions

Benefits

  • Clear operational history
  • Improved decision‑making
  • Support for investigations
  • Compliance and audit readiness

Access control

Timeline visibility is governed by RBAC. Sensitive events may be restricted to HR or Security roles.

Audit integrity

Timeline entries are derived from immutable audit and lifecycle data, ensuring accuracy and defensibility.
