Engine Overview

The Risk Model Engine evaluates organizational risk using a weighted scoring model that incorporates incident severity, case outcomes, readiness gaps, behavioral patterns, and location‑level indicators.

Risk inputs

  • Incident frequency and severity
  • Case findings and corrective actions
  • Readiness score trends
  • Employee behavior indicators
  • Location and department risk profiles

Operational impact

Risk scores influence dashboards, alerts, and leadership reporting. High‑risk areas may trigger targeted interventions or compliance reviews.

Audit integrity

All risk calculations and contributing factors are logged for transparency and defensibility.
