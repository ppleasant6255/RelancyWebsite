Anonymity Overview

Anonymous reporting allows employees to submit incidents without revealing their identity. Relancy enforces strict anonymity rules across storage, routing, and audit layers.

Protection rules

  • No identity fields stored
  • No IP or device metadata retained
  • Anonymous routing paths
  • Restricted access to anonymous submissions

Operational safeguards

Anonymous reports cannot be linked to employees through metadata, behavior patterns, or system logs.

Audit integrity

Audit logs record submission events without identity or traceable metadata, ensuring true anonymity.
