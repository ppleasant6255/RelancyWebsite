Training Lineage and Readiness Tracking

How Relancy maintains a complete, immutable record of training and readiness.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Training lineage is Relancy’s record of every training assignment, completion, expiration, and renewal. It forms the foundation of readiness tracking and ensures that organizations can demonstrate compliance and preparedness at any moment.

What training lineage includes

  • Assignment date
  • Completion date
  • Expiration date
  • Renewal history
  • Delivery method
  • Scoring or assessment results

These records are immutable and preserved for the life of the tenant.

Readiness tracking

Readiness is calculated based on training lineage, role requirements, and organizational policies. Relancy provides:

  • Individual readiness status
  • Team and department readiness summaries
  • Organization-wide readiness dashboards

Why lineage is immutable

Training lineage cannot be edited or deleted. This ensures:

  • Regulatory compliance
  • Accurate historical reporting
  • Defensible audit trails
  • Reliable readiness calculations

Integrations

External training systems can push completion data into Relancy through safe ingestion endpoints. These endpoints do not allow modification of historical lineage—only the creation of new entries.
