Organizational Policy Mapping

How Relancy aligns cases, training, and readiness with organizational policies and standards.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Policy mapping connects operational workflows to organizational standards, ensuring that cases, training, and readiness align with documented policies. Relancy provides structured tools to map policies to operational entities.

Policy types

  • HR policies
  • Safety policies
  • Security protocols
  • Compliance requirements
  • Training standards

Mapping workflows

  • Link policies to case categories
  • Associate training modules with policy requirements
  • Map corrective actions to policy violations
  • Align readiness scoring with policy compliance

Audit and reporting

Policy mappings appear in reports and dashboards, helping organizations demonstrate compliance during audits.

Governance

Policy updates are logged in the audit trail. Only authorized administrators may modify policy mappings.
