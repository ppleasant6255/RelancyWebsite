Status Overview

Relancy provides real‑time platform status updates, uptime guarantees, and scheduled maintenance notifications. Status transparency supports operational planning and tenant trust.

Uptime guarantees

  • Redundant infrastructure
  • 99.9% uptime target for SaaS tenants
  • Custom SLAs for Private Deployment

Status communication

  • Public status page
  • In‑app notifications for outages
  • Scheduled maintenance announcements
  • Post‑incident summaries

Operational impact

Maintenance windows are designed to minimize disruption. Critical workflows remain available whenever possible.
