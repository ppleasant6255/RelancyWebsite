Employee Role Change Governance

How Relancy manages role transitions to maintain readiness, reporting accuracy, and audit integrity.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Role changes affect training requirements, readiness scoring, case visibility, and reporting. Relancy enforces governance rules to ensure role transitions are handled consistently and safely.

Role change triggers

  • Promotion or demotion
  • Department transfer
  • Location change
  • Shift or team reassignment

Governance controls

  • Automatic training reassignment
  • Readiness recalculation
  • Case visibility updates
  • Manager hierarchy updates

Audit logging

All role changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification.

Compliance alignment

Role change governance supports regulatory requirements for training, safety, and HR documentation.
