Closure Overview

Case closure requires complete documentation, evidence review, and verification of corrective actions. Relancy enforces closure standards to ensure consistency, defensibility, and compliance alignment.

Closure requirements

  • All required lifecycle stages completed
  • Evidence reviewed and validated
  • Corrective actions completed or assigned
  • Findings documented clearly and neutrally
  • Classification confirmed

Closure notes

Closure notes must summarize findings, decisions, and any follow‑up actions. Notes must be factual, neutral, and free of speculation.

Audit integrity

Closure events are logged with timestamp, actor, and justification. Closed cases remain read‑only unless reopened under governance rules.
