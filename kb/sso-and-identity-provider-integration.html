Integration Overview

Relancy supports enterprise-grade authentication through SSO integrations with major identity providers. Authentication is secure, token-based, and fully auditable.

Supported protocols

  • SAML 2.0
  • OAuth 2.0
  • OpenID Connect

Operational safeguards

SSO configurations require certificate validation, metadata verification, and strict redirect URI controls.

Audit integrity

Authentication events include timestamp, identity provider, token metadata, and session details for compliance review.
