Data Quality and Validation Rules

How Relancy enforces data accuracy, consistency, and integrity across all entities.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

High‑quality data is essential for accurate reporting, readiness scoring, and risk detection. Relancy enforces validation rules to ensure data remains consistent and reliable.

Validation categories

  • Required fields
  • Format validation (dates, IDs, emails)
  • Duplicate detection
  • Cross‑entity consistency checks
  • Lifecycle‑based validation

Import validation

All imports undergo strict validation before being accepted. Errors are logged and must be corrected before re‑submission.

Operational benefits

  • Improved reporting accuracy
  • Reduced investigation errors
  • Stronger readiness scoring
  • Better risk detection

Audit logging

Validation failures and corrections are logged for transparency and compliance.
