MFA Overview

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) adds an additional layer of security by requiring a second verification factor during login. Relancy enforces MFA through identity provider policies.

Supported MFA methods

  • Authenticator apps
  • SMS codes
  • Hardware tokens
  • Biometric verification (via IdP)

Operational safeguards

MFA cannot be bypassed for privileged roles. Failed MFA attempts trigger alerts and temporary lockouts.

Audit integrity

MFA events include timestamp, method used, and identity provider metadata for compliance alignment.
