Training Requirements by Role

How Relancy assigns and tracks training based on job responsibilities.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Training requirements vary across roles, departments, and locations. Relancy allows organizations to define role-based training requirements that feed directly into readiness scoring and compliance reporting.

Role-based training assignments

Administrators can assign required training to roles such as:

  • Managers
  • Security Officers
  • HR Administrators
  • Frontline employees
  • Supervisors
  • Specialized roles (e.g., forklift operators)

Automatic assignment

When an employee is assigned a role, Relancy automatically assigns the required training and updates readiness status accordingly.

Expiration and renewal

Role-based training may include expiration dates and renewal cycles. Relancy tracks these through training lineage.

Reporting

Role-based training compliance is included in readiness dashboards and exports.
