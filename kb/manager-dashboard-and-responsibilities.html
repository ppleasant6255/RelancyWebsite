Dashboard Overview

The Manager Dashboard provides a consolidated view of team readiness, training compliance, incidents, and corrective actions. It supports proactive leadership and compliance alignment.

Manager responsibilities

  • Review team incidents and cases (as permitted)
  • Monitor training and readiness gaps
  • Approve or escalate corrective actions
  • Respond to overdue tasks
  • Support organizational reporting requirements

Access boundaries

Managers only see data for their direct reports and organizational scope. Sensitive HR or Security cases may be restricted.

Operational integration

Manager actions feed into readiness scoring, reporting, and escalation workflows.
