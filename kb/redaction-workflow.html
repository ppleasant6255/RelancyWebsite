Workflow Overview

The Redaction Workflow ensures that sensitive information is removed or masked before sharing, exporting, or presenting case data. Redactions follow strict rules and require justification.

Redaction types

  • Identity redaction
  • Protected‑class redaction
  • Confidential note exclusion
  • Evidence metadata redaction

Operational safeguards

Redactions cannot be reversed without elevated permissions. All redacted versions are stored separately from originals.

Audit integrity

Redaction events include timestamp, actor, justification, and affected fields for defensibility and compliance alignment.
