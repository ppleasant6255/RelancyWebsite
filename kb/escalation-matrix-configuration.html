Matrix Overview

The escalation matrix defines who is notified and when based on severity, category, and organizational policy. It ensures timely response and leadership visibility for high‑risk events.

Configurable elements

  • Severity thresholds
  • Category‑specific escalation paths
  • Location‑based escalation rules
  • Leadership notification triggers
  • Corrective action escalation rules

Operational impact

Escalation ensures that high‑risk events receive immediate attention and that leadership remains informed of critical issues.

Audit integrity

All escalation events and configuration changes are logged for compliance and historical traceability.
