Framework Overview

The Recurrence Prevention Framework analyzes incidents and cases to identify root causes, contributing factors, and systemic issues. It recommends targeted interventions to prevent recurrence.

Prevention components

  • Root cause analysis
  • Corrective action alignment
  • Training and readiness interventions
  • Policy updates
  • Environmental or process changes

Operational impact

Prevention reduces incident frequency, improves safety culture, and strengthens compliance posture.

Audit integrity

Prevention recommendations and implemented changes are logged for transparency and defensibility.
