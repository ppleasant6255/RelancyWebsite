Module Overview

The Reporting module provides configurable reports across incidents, cases, readiness, training, and risk. Reports support leadership reviews, compliance audits, and operational decision‑making.

Report categories

  • Incident trend reports
  • Case lifecycle performance
  • Training compliance
  • Readiness distribution
  • Corrective action status
  • Location risk profiles

Filtering and segmentation

Reports can be filtered by date range, department, location, severity, category, and role.

Export options

Reports may be exported for leadership presentations, compliance audits, or offline analysis.

RBAC enforcement

Users only see data they are authorized to access, ensuring confidentiality and compliance.
