Overview

The case timeline provides a unified, chronological view of all activity within a case. This includes lifecycle transitions, notes, attachments, assignments, and related incidents or investigations.

Timeline components

  • Case creation and assignment
  • Lifecycle transitions
  • Notes and protected notes
  • Attachments and evidence uploads
  • Investigation milestones
  • Corrective actions
  • Linked incidents or cases

Benefits

  • Clear operational narrative
  • Improved audit readiness
  • Faster case reviews
  • Better cross‑team collaboration

Access control

Timeline visibility is governed by RBAC. Sensitive entries may be restricted to HR or Security roles.

Audit integrity

Timeline entries are derived from immutable audit data, ensuring accuracy and defensibility.
