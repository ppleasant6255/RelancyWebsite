Pattern Detection Overview

Relancy analyzes incident and case histories to identify behavioral patterns such as repeat behavior, escalating severity, or cross‑location trends. Patterns support proactive HR and Security intervention.

Pattern types

  • Repeat behavior by an employee
  • Recurring incidents in a location
  • Escalating severity patterns
  • Cross‑departmental trends
  • Policy violation clusters

Operational impact

Detected patterns may trigger alerts, escalations, or targeted training assignments.

Privacy safeguards

Pattern detection respects RBAC and does not expose sensitive data to unauthorized users.

Audit integrity

Pattern detection events and contributing factors are logged for transparency and defensibility.
