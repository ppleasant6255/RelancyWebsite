Algorithm Overview

Severity scoring algorithms evaluate incident and case severity using weighted factors such as harm potential, policy violations, repeat behavior, and environmental risk.

Scoring factors

  • Impact on individuals
  • Operational disruption
  • Regulatory implications
  • Repeat behavior indicators
  • Location‑specific risk multipliers

Operational impact

Severity scores influence routing, escalation, corrective actions, and leadership reporting.

Audit integrity

All scoring events include timestamp, actor, and contributing factors for defensibility and compliance alignment.
