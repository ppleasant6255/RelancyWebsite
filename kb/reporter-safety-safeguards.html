Safety Overview

Reporter Safety Safeguards ensure that individuals who submit incidents—anonymous or identified—are protected from retaliation and unauthorized visibility.

Safety mechanisms

  • Restricted visibility of reporter identity
  • Automatic conflict‑of‑interest routing
  • Redaction of reporter details in exports
  • Privileged access for HR/Security only

Operational safeguards

Reporter identity cannot be viewed by managers or investigators unless explicitly authorized by RBAC rules.

Audit integrity

All access to reporter identity is logged with timestamp, actor, and justification for compliance review.
