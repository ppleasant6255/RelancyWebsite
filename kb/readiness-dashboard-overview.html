Readiness Dashboard Overview

How Relancy visualizes organizational preparedness across roles, departments, and locations.

Overview

The readiness dashboard provides a real‑time view of training compliance and preparedness across the organization. It highlights gaps, upcoming expirations, and areas requiring immediate attention.

Dashboard components

  • Overall readiness score
  • Department‑level readiness
  • Role‑based compliance
  • Upcoming training expirations
  • Overdue training items
  • Readiness trends over time

Operational benefits

  • Proactive training management
  • Improved compliance
  • Leadership visibility
  • Targeted interventions

Export options

Dashboard data can be exported for internal reporting or compliance audits.

Access control

Managers and leaders see only their teams’ readiness data, while administrators may view organization‑wide metrics.
