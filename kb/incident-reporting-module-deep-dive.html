Module Overview

The Incident Reporting module provides structured intake forms, classification rules, and routing logic to ensure incidents are captured accurately and handled promptly.

Core capabilities

  • Incident intake forms
  • Category and severity classification
  • Auto‑routing to HR or Security
  • Attachment and evidence capture
  • Witness management

Classification rules

Incidents are classified using a structured taxonomy and may trigger severity auto‑classification or escalation.

Routing logic

Routing is based on category, severity, location, and organizational policy.

Audit logging

All incident submissions and updates are logged for compliance and review.
