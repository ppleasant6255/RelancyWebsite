Training Content Governance

How Relancy ensures training content is accurate, compliant, and aligned with organizational policy.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Training content plays a critical role in readiness and compliance. Relancy enforces governance rules to ensure that training materials are accurate, up‑to‑date, and aligned with organizational standards.

Content types

  • Online modules
  • Instructor‑led materials
  • Policy documents
  • Video‑based training
  • Assessment content

Governance controls

  • Version control
  • Approval workflows
  • Expiration and review cycles
  • Restricted editing permissions

Readiness integration

Training content versions are tied to readiness scoring to ensure employees complete the correct materials.

Audit logging

All content updates, approvals, and retirements are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
