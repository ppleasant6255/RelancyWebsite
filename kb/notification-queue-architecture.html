Architecture Overview

The Notification Queue Architecture uses distributed, tenant‑scoped queues to process email, SMS, and in‑app notifications. Queues ensure reliable delivery, retry logic, and isolation between tenants.

Queue components

  • Tenant‑scoped notification queues
  • Retry queues for transient failures
  • Dead‑letter queues for unrecoverable messages
  • Delivery workers with horizontal scaling

Operational safeguards

Notification queues enforce rate limits, validate payloads, and prevent cross‑tenant contamination through strict scoping and encryption.

Audit integrity

Notification events—including enqueue, dequeue, retry, and failure—are logged for compliance and operational review.
