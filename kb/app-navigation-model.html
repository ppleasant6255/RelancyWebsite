Navigation Overview

Relancy uses a structured navigation model designed for clarity, speed, and operational consistency. Navigation is organized around functional modules, contextual actions, and deep links that take users directly to relevant records.

Primary navigation

  • Dashboard
  • Cases
  • Incidents
  • Investigations
  • Employees
  • Training & Readiness
  • Reports
  • Admin Center

Contextual navigation

Contextual links appear throughout the app, allowing users to jump directly to related entities such as linked cases, employees, or evidence.

Deep linking

Relancy supports deep links that open specific cases, incidents, or dashboards directly from notifications or external systems.

Back navigation

The app maintains a consistent back‑stack to ensure users can return to previous screens without losing context.
