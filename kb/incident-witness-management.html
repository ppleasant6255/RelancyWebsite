Incident Witness Management

How Relancy tracks and manages witness statements during incident and investigation workflows.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Witness statements are a critical component of incident and investigation workflows. Relancy provides structured tools to capture, store, and audit witness information securely.

Witness information

  • Name and contact details
  • Relationship to the incident
  • Statement or interview summary
  • Attachments or supporting evidence

Access control

Witness information is restricted to authorized HR and Security roles. Sensitive statements may be further restricted.

Audit logging

All witness entries, edits, and views are logged in the audit trail.

Integration with investigations

Witness statements are linked to investigation findings and may influence corrective actions or root cause analysis.
