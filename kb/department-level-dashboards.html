Department-Level Dashboards

How Relancy provides insights into departmental readiness, incidents, and risk patterns.

Overview

Department-level dashboards give leaders visibility into operational performance, readiness, and risk trends within their teams. These dashboards help identify gaps, allocate resources, and support proactive action.

Dashboard components

  • Incident volume and trends
  • Case severity distribution
  • Training completion rates
  • Readiness scores
  • Risk signals and patterns
  • Corrective action status

Role-based visibility

Managers and department leaders see only data relevant to their teams, ensuring privacy and compliance.

Operational benefits

  • Identify high-risk areas
  • Monitor training gaps
  • Track case resolution performance
  • Support compliance reporting

Export options

Dashboard data can be exported for offline analysis or leadership reporting.
