Metrics Overview

Investigator Performance Metrics provide insight into investigation quality, timeliness, and compliance alignment. Metrics support coaching, workload planning, and operational excellence.

Performance indicators

  • Case resolution time
  • Escalation frequency
  • Corrective action follow‑through
  • Case outcome quality
  • Documentation completeness

Operational safeguards

Metrics exclude protected‑class data and cannot be used for punitive decisions without human review.

Audit integrity

All performance calculations and contributing datasets are logged for transparency and defensibility.
