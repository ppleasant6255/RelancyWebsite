Offboarding Overview

Offboarding workflows ensure that departing employees are removed from active workflows, reassigned from open cases, and processed according to compliance requirements.

Offboarding tasks

  • Access removal
  • Case reassignment
  • Corrective action review
  • Certification and training archival
  • Policy acknowledgment retention

Operational impact

Offboarding ensures that workflows remain accurate and that no open tasks remain assigned to inactive users.

Audit integrity

All offboarding events are logged for compliance and historical traceability.
