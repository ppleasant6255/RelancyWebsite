Notification and Escalation Rules

How Relancy ensures the right people are informed at the right time.

Overview

Relancy uses structured notification and escalation rules to ensure timely response to incidents, cases, and investigations. These rules help organizations maintain operational awareness and meet compliance obligations.

Notification types

  • Lifecycle notifications: Triggered when cases or incidents change state.
  • Assignment notifications: Sent when a user is assigned responsibility.
  • Training notifications: Alerts for assignments, completions, and overdue items.
  • Risk notifications: Triggered by pattern detection or severity changes.

Escalation rules

Escalations occur when a case or incident meets specific criteria, such as:

  • High or critical severity
  • Missed response deadlines
  • Repeated incidents involving the same individual
  • Policy-defined escalation triggers

Delivery channels

Notifications may be delivered via:

  • Email
  • In-app alerts
  • Webhook events

Audit logging

All notifications and escalations are logged in the audit trail, including delivery status and recipient details.
