Engine Overview

The Webhook Delivery Engine sends event notifications to external systems when incidents, cases, training, or readiness events occur. Delivery is asynchronous, secure, and fully auditable.

Delivery features

  • Tenant‑scoped webhook endpoints
  • HMAC signature validation
  • Automatic retries with exponential backoff
  • Dead‑letter queue for failed deliveries

Operational safeguards

Payloads are sanitized, encrypted in transit, and validated before delivery. Failed endpoints are temporarily disabled to protect system stability.

Audit integrity

All webhook attempts—including payload, status, and retry count—are logged for compliance and troubleshooting.
