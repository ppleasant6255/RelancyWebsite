Protocol Overview

Crisis communication ensures rapid, accurate, and targeted messaging during emergencies such as workplace violence, environmental hazards, or critical safety failures.

Protocol stages

  • Incident detection
  • Leadership approval
  • Rapid broadcast delivery
  • Follow‑up instructions
  • Post‑crisis review

Operational safeguards

Crisis messages bypass standard queues but require elevated approval. Delivery is prioritized across all channels.

Audit integrity

Crisis communications are logged with timestamp, sender, approval chain, and delivery status for regulatory review.
