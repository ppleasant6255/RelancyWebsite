Framework Overview

The Predictive Analytics Framework analyzes historical incidents, cases, readiness scores, and behavioral patterns to forecast emerging risks and operational trends.

Predictive capabilities

  • Incident trend forecasting
  • Location‑level risk prediction
  • Readiness decline forecasting
  • Corrective action completion likelihood
  • Behavioral pattern emergence

Operational safeguards

Predictive models are transparent, explainable, and never used for disciplinary decisions without human review.

Audit integrity

Model inputs, outputs, and version history are logged for compliance and governance oversight.
