Employee Acknowledgment Tracking

Tracking Overview

Employee acknowledgments confirm that individuals have reviewed policies, completed training, or received safety communications. Tracking is automated and auditable.

Acknowledgment types

  • Policy acknowledgments
  • Training completions
  • Safety communication confirmations
  • Corrective action attestations

Operational safeguards

Acknowledgments require explicit employee action. Backdating or proxy acknowledgments are prohibited.

Audit integrity

All acknowledgments include timestamp, actor, version metadata, and delivery method for compliance alignment.
