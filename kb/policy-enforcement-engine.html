Engine Overview

The Policy Enforcement Engine ensures that organizational rules are consistently applied across all workflows. It evaluates policies during case creation, incident intake, training assignment, and readiness scoring.

Policy types

  • Case routing rules
  • Severity classification rules
  • Training requirements
  • Readiness scoring rules
  • Confidentiality and access rules

Evaluation behavior

Policies are evaluated in real time. Conflicts are resolved using a deterministic priority model.

Audit logging

All policy evaluations and overrides are logged for compliance and review.

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may define custom policy engines or integrate external rule systems.
