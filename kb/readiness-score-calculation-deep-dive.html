Readiness Overview

Readiness scores reflect an employee’s preparedness to perform their role safely and effectively. Scores are calculated using a weighted model that incorporates training, certifications, and role requirements.

Score components

  • Training completions
  • Certification status
  • Role‑based requirements
  • Department‑level requirements
  • Expiration cycles

Weighting model

Each component contributes a percentage to the overall score. Weighting is configurable by administrators to reflect organizational priorities.

Operational impact

Readiness scores influence dashboards, reporting, and training assignments. Low scores may trigger alerts or corrective actions.

Audit integrity

All score calculations and contributing factors are logged for transparency and defensibility.
