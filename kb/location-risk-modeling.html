Risk Model Overview

Location risk modeling aggregates incident trends, severity distribution, readiness gaps, and environmental factors to generate a location‑level risk score.

Risk factors

  • Incident frequency and severity
  • Repeat behavior patterns
  • Training and readiness coverage
  • Corrective action performance
  • Environmental or operational hazards

Operational impact

High‑risk locations may trigger escalations, targeted training, or leadership review.

Reporting

Location risk scores appear in dashboards, exports, and leadership reports.

Audit integrity

All risk calculations are logged with contributing factors for transparency and defensibility.
