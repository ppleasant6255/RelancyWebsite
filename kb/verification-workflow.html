Workflow Overview

Verification ensures that corrective actions and case outcomes are completed accurately and meet organizational and regulatory standards. Verification is mandatory for high‑risk actions.

Verification steps

  • Evidence submission
  • Reviewer assignment
  • Evidence evaluation
  • Approval or rejection
  • Final audit logging

Operational safeguards

Verification cannot be performed by the same person who completed the action. Conflicts of interest are automatically flagged.

Audit integrity

Verification events include timestamp, reviewer identity, evidence, and justification for approval or rejection.
