Relationship Overview

Relancy is designed as a unified platform, not a collection of disconnected modules. Data relationships are modeled explicitly to preserve context and support cross‑module insights.

Key relationships

  • Incidents feeding into HR or Security cases
  • Cases and investigations linked to corrective actions
  • Training and readiness linked to employees and roles
  • Location and department structures driving visibility and routing
  • Risk and severity scores aggregating across modules

Reporting impact

Cross‑module relationships enable holistic reporting, allowing leadership to see how incidents, training, readiness, and corrective actions interact over time.

Audit integrity

Relationships are fully auditable, ensuring that cross‑module insights remain defensible.
