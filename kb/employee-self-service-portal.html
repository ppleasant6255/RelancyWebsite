Portal Overview

The Employee Self‑Service Portal provides employees with access to training assignments, readiness status, reporting tools, and personal information. Access is governed by RBAC and tenant policy.

Available features

  • Training assignments and completions
  • Readiness score overview
  • Incident reporting tools
  • Policy acknowledgments
  • Personal profile updates (limited)

Access boundaries

Employees may only view their own data and assigned training. Sensitive HR or Security information is never visible.

Mobile experience

The portal is optimized for mobile devices, enabling employees to complete training or submit reports on the go.

Audit logging

All portal interactions are logged for compliance and operational review.
