ONA Overview

Organizational Network Analysis (ONA) evaluates how employees interact across incidents, cases, corrective actions, and readiness workflows. ONA highlights collaboration patterns, bottlenecks, and risk clusters.

ONA indicators

  • Cross‑department collaboration frequency
  • Managerial workload distribution
  • Investigator case routing patterns
  • Corrective action dependency networks
  • Location‑level communication density

Operational impact

ONA insights support leadership decisions, resource allocation, and early detection of structural risks.

Audit integrity

ONA calculations and underlying datasets are logged for transparency and compliance alignment.
