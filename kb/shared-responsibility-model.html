HR & Security Shared Responsibility Model

How HR and Security collaborate within Relancy’s operational framework.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy supports a shared responsibility model between HR and Security teams. Each group plays a distinct role in maintaining a safe, compliant, and operationally sound environment. This article outlines those responsibilities and how the platform supports them.

HR responsibilities

  • Managing employee records
  • Assigning and tracking training
  • Monitoring readiness
  • Handling sensitive personnel matters
  • Ensuring compliance with employment policies

Security responsibilities

  • Responding to incidents
  • Conducting investigations
  • Managing risk signals
  • Coordinating with HR on cross-functional cases
  • Maintaining operational safety

Shared responsibilities

  • Case management
  • Incident escalation
  • Corrective action planning
  • Readiness and compliance reporting

Platform support

Relancy enforces clear boundaries through role-based access, lifecycle controls, and audit logging. This ensures that HR and Security collaborate effectively without overstepping responsibilities.
