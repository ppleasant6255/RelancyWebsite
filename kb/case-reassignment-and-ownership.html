Case Reassignment and Ownership

How Relancy manages responsibility for cases across HR and Security teams.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Case ownership determines who is responsible for managing a case through its lifecycle. Relancy supports structured assignment, reassignment, and multi-role collaboration while preserving audit integrity.

Ownership model

Each case has a primary owner responsible for:

  • Lifecycle transitions
  • Documentation
  • Communication
  • Corrective actions

Reassignment rules

Cases may be reassigned when:

  • Expertise is required
  • Ownership changes due to role transitions
  • Escalation requires leadership involvement

Reassignments are logged in the audit trail.

Multi-role collaboration

HR and Security may collaborate on cases. RBAC ensures each role sees only what is appropriate.

Notifications

Owners receive notifications for lifecycle changes, assignments, and corrective actions.
