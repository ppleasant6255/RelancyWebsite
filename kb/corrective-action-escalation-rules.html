Escalation Overview

Corrective actions ensure that findings and risks are addressed promptly. Relancy escalates overdue or high‑severity actions to ensure accountability and timely resolution.

Escalation triggers

  • Overdue corrective actions
  • High‑severity findings
  • Repeat behavior indicators
  • Location or department risk thresholds

Escalation paths

  • Manager notification
  • HR or Security escalation
  • Leadership escalation for critical issues
  • Compliance team notification

Audit integrity

All escalation events are logged with timestamp, actor, and triggering conditions for defensibility and compliance alignment.
