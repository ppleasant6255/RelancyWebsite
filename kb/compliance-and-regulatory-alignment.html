Overview

Relancy is designed to help organizations meet compliance obligations related to safety, HR, investigations, training, and data privacy. The platform enforces lifecycle discipline, audit integrity, and data isolation to support regulatory alignment.

Key compliance features

  • Immutable audit logs: Every action is recorded with full context.
  • Lifecycle enforcement: Required steps cannot be bypassed.
  • Training lineage: Complete historical record of training activity.
  • Data isolation: Tenant-specific storage and encryption.
  • Access control: Role-based permissions and need-to-know restrictions.

Supported regulatory areas

Relancy supports compliance efforts in areas such as:

  • Workplace safety
  • HR investigations
  • Anti-discrimination and protected-class handling
  • Training and certification requirements
  • Data privacy and retention

Reporting and documentation

Built-in reports and exports help organizations demonstrate compliance during audits, reviews, and regulatory inquiries.

Private Deployment considerations

Organizations with strict regulatory requirements may choose Private Deployment for dedicated infrastructure, custom SLAs, and extended control.
