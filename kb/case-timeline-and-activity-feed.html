Timeline Overview

The case timeline provides a chronological view of all actions, notes, evidence uploads, assignments, and lifecycle transitions. It supports defensibility and operational clarity.

Timeline components

  • Case creation
  • Classification changes
  • Assignments and escalations
  • Notes and protected notes
  • Evidence uploads
  • Lifecycle transitions
  • Closure events

Audit integrity

Timeline entries are immutable and reflect the full audit history of the case.
