Data Residency and Sovereignty

How Relancy ensures tenant data remains within required geographic and regulatory boundaries.

Overview

Many organizations require that data remain within specific geographic regions. Relancy supports data residency and sovereignty requirements through region‑specific hosting and strict isolation controls.

Residency guarantees

  • Tenant data stored only in the selected region
  • No cross‑region replication without explicit approval
  • Region‑specific encryption keys

Supported regions

Relancy offers multiple hosting regions. Availability may vary by plan or regulatory requirement.

Compliance alignment

  • Data privacy regulations
  • Industry‑specific requirements
  • Government or contractual mandates

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may host Relancy in their own region‑specific infrastructure.
