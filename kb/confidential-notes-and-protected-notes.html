Note Types Overview

Relancy supports standard notes, confidential notes, and protected notes. Each type has different visibility rules and audit requirements to ensure sensitive information is handled appropriately.

Standard notes

Visible to authorized HR, Security, and managers depending on case type and policy.

Confidential notes

  • Restricted to HR or Security roles
  • Not visible to managers or employees
  • Used for sensitive details requiring limited access

Protected notes

  • Restricted to designated senior HR or Security roles
  • Used for protected‑class information or legal matters
  • Require justification for creation

Audit logging

All note creation, edits, and access events are logged with full detail for defensibility.
