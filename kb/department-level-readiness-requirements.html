Department‑Level Readiness Requirements

How Relancy supports readiness standards tailored to specific departments or operational units.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Different departments may require different training, certifications, or readiness standards. Relancy allows organizations to define department‑specific readiness requirements that feed into scoring and reporting.

Department‑specific requirements

  • Mandatory training modules
  • Certification requirements
  • Role‑specific readiness criteria
  • Location‑based requirements

Automatic assignment

When an employee joins a department, Relancy automatically assigns the required training and updates readiness scoring.

Reporting

Department‑level readiness dashboards highlight gaps and upcoming expirations.

Audit logging

All requirement changes are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
