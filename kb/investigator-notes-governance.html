Investigator Notes Governance

How Relancy governs investigator notes using immutability, confidentiality rules, and audit logging.

Back to Knowledge Base

Governance Overview

Investigator notes are treated as sensitive, immutable records. Notes support case findings, evidence interpretation, and legal defensibility.

Governance rules

  • Notes are immutable once saved
  • Edits require justification and elevated permissions
  • Confidential notes follow restricted visibility rules
  • Notes are excluded from standard exports unless authorized

Operational safeguards

Notes cannot contain protected‑class data unless required for legal or compliance purposes.

Audit integrity

All note creation, edits, and access events are logged with timestamp, actor, and confidentiality level.
