Witness Overview

Witnesses provide critical context for incidents and investigations. Relancy ensures that witness information is handled confidentially and stored with full audit integrity.

Witness data elements

  • Witness identity (optional)
  • Statement or testimony
  • Linked incident or case
  • Confidentiality level
  • Attachments or evidence

Confidentiality levels

  • Standard confidentiality
  • Restricted HR‑only visibility
  • Anonymous witness (identity withheld)

Audit integrity

Witness statements and access events are logged to ensure defensibility and compliance with policy.
