Analytics Overview

Usage analytics provide insights into tenant activity, adoption trends, and operational performance. Analytics are tenant‑scoped and never include cross‑tenant data.

Tracked metrics

  • User activity and login frequency
  • Incident and case volume
  • Training and readiness engagement
  • Corrective action throughput
  • Notification delivery metrics

Operational impact

Analytics help administrators identify adoption gaps, training needs, and workflow bottlenecks.

Audit integrity

Analytics generation events are logged for transparency and compliance alignment.
