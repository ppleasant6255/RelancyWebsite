Workflow Overview

The Evidence Request Workflow enables investigators to request additional evidence from employees, managers, or external parties. Requests are tracked and verified for completeness.

Workflow stages

  • Request creation and justification
  • Recipient notification
  • Evidence submission
  • Validation and classification
  • Chain‑of‑custody logging

Operational safeguards

Sensitive evidence requires elevated permissions and cannot be viewed by unauthorized roles.

Audit integrity

All evidence requests and submissions are logged with timestamp, actor, and classification metadata.
