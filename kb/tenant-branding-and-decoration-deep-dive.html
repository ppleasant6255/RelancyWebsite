Branding Overview

Tenants may customize branding elements such as logos, colors, and contextual panels. Relancy enforces strict boundaries to ensure branding does not affect platform behavior or compromise security.

Customizable elements

  • Tenant logo
  • Primary and secondary color themes
  • Dashboard header decoration
  • HTML contextual panels (safe subset)

HTML panel safety

HTML panels support a restricted subset of HTML and CSS. Scripts, external resources, and unsafe tags are blocked to prevent cross‑tenant contamination or security risks.

Governance

Administrators manage branding settings. All changes are logged and versioned for audit purposes.

Private Deployment

Private Deployment tenants may enable extended branding options within their isolated environment.
