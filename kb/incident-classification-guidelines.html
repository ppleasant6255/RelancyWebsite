Incident Classification Guidelines

How to classify incidents consistently and accurately within Relancy.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Accurate incident classification ensures consistent reporting, risk analysis, and case routing. Relancy provides structured guidelines to help users classify incidents correctly.

Classification principles

  • Classify based on observed facts
  • Use the most specific applicable category
  • Avoid assumptions or speculation
  • Update classification if new information emerges

Common classification errors

  • Over‑generalizing incident types
  • Misclassifying due to incomplete information
  • Using categories inconsistently across teams

Governance

Administrators may periodically review classifications to ensure consistency and accuracy.

Reporting impact

Classification accuracy directly affects dashboards, risk detection, and compliance reporting.
