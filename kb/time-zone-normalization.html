Normalization Overview

Relancy normalizes timestamps to ensure consistent interpretation across global teams. All timestamps are stored in UTC and converted to local time zones for display.

Normalization rules

  • All stored timestamps use UTC
  • User interface displays local time zone
  • Exports include both UTC and local time
  • Audit logs preserve original time zone context

Operational impact

Time zone normalization ensures accurate reporting, reduces confusion, and supports global compliance reviews.

Audit integrity

All timestamp conversions and time zone changes are logged for transparency and defensibility.
