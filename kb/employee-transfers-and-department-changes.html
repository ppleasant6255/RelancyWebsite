Employee Transfers and Department Changes

How Relancy handles employee movement across departments, locations, and roles.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Employee transfers affect readiness, reporting, and case visibility. Relancy tracks department and role changes to maintain accurate operational context and audit history.

Transfer scenarios

  • Department changes
  • Location transfers
  • Role promotions or reassignments
  • Manager or supervisor changes

Impact on readiness

Training requirements may change when an employee moves to a new role or department. Relancy automatically updates readiness scoring based on new requirements.

Case visibility impact

Transfers may affect which cases a manager or leader can see. Historical visibility is preserved in the audit trail.

Audit logging

All transfers and department changes are logged with timestamp, actor, and reason.
