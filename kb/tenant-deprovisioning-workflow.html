Deprovisioning Overview

Tenant deprovisioning removes access, disables workflows, and securely purges or archives tenant data according to retention and legal requirements.

Deprovisioning steps

  • Disable tenant access
  • Revoke authentication and API keys
  • Archive or purge data based on policy
  • Remove storage partitions
  • Revoke encryption keys

Operational safeguards

Deprovisioning is irreversible and validated to ensure no residual data or access remains.

Audit integrity

All deprovisioning events are logged with timestamp, actor, and affected resources for compliance review.
