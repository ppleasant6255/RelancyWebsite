Workflow Overview

Policy acknowledgment workflows ensure that employees receive, review, and acknowledge required policies. Acknowledgments support compliance and organizational governance.

Workflow steps

  • Policy distribution to relevant employees
  • Employee review and acknowledgment
  • Manager oversight for overdue acknowledgments
  • Compliance reporting

Operational impact

Acknowledgments may influence readiness scores, training assignments, or compliance dashboards.

Audit integrity

All acknowledgment events are logged with timestamp, actor, and policy version for defensibility.
