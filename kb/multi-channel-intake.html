Channel Overview

Relancy supports incident intake through web, mobile app, kiosk mode, and hotline transcription. All channels follow the same validation and routing rules.

Channel features

  • Web: full intake form with attachments
  • Mobile: streamlined intake with camera evidence
  • Kiosk: anonymous‑first intake with session isolation
  • Hotline: transcribed submissions with metadata controls

Operational safeguards

All channels normalize data into a unified intake format to ensure consistent routing and reporting.

Audit integrity

Channel metadata is logged without storing sensitive device identifiers or personal information.
