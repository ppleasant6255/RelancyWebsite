Scheduling Overview

Interviews are a core component of investigations. Relancy provides tools to schedule interviews, track their status, and ensure all participants are notified and prepared.

Scheduling capabilities

  • Schedule interviews from the case or incident detail page
  • Assign interviewer and interviewee from linked persons
  • Set date, time, location, and estimated duration
  • Attach preparation notes or question templates
  • Track interview status (Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled, Rescheduled)

Coordination rules

  • Interviewers must have Investigator or Supervisor role on the case
  • Interviewees are linked as Persons and scoped to the tenant
  • Scheduling conflicts are flagged when the same person has overlapping interviews
  • Notifications are sent to all participants when an interview is created or modified

Post-interview workflow

  • Attach interview notes or recordings as evidence
  • Link interview outcomes to case findings
  • Mark follow-up interviews if additional information is needed

Audit integrity

All interview scheduling actions, status changes, and note attachments are logged with full tenant, user, and timestamp context to ensure defensibility.
