Metrics Overview

Knowledge retention metrics evaluate how well employees retain training content over time. Metrics combine assessment performance, readiness trends, and behavioral indicators.

Retention indicators

  • Assessment score decay
  • Readiness score stability
  • Corrective action recurrence
  • Behavioral pattern shifts
  • Training refresh compliance

Operational safeguards

Retention metrics cannot be used for disciplinary decisions. They support coaching and training improvements.

Audit integrity

Metric calculations and underlying datasets are logged for transparency and defensibility.
