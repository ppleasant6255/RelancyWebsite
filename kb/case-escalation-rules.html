Escalation Overview

Case Escalation Rules ensure that high‑risk or sensitive cases are routed to the appropriate HR/Security leaders or specialized investigators.

Escalation triggers

  • High severity or imminent harm
  • Protected‑class involvement
  • Repeat behavior patterns
  • Location‑level risk spikes
  • Investigator conflict‑of‑interest

Operational safeguards

Escalations require justification and cannot be reversed without elevated permissions and audit review.

Audit integrity

All escalation events include timestamp, actor, justification, and routing metadata for defensibility.
