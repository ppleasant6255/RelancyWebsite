Redaction Overview

Redaction workflows allow authorized users to remove or mask sensitive information while preserving audit integrity. Redactions are versioned, reversible by administrators, and fully logged.

Redactable elements

  • Case notes
  • Incident descriptions
  • Interview transcripts
  • Evidence attachments
  • Exported reports

Redaction process

  • Authorized user selects content to redact
  • Redacted version replaces original in UI
  • Original content remains preserved in audit history
  • Redaction event is logged with justification

Compliance alignment

Redaction supports privacy regulations, legal hold requirements, and internal confidentiality policies.

Audit integrity

Redactions never delete original content; they mask it while preserving full audit traceability.
