Evidence Overview

Evidence management is a core component of Relancy’s HR and Security workflows. The platform ensures that all evidence is captured securely, stored with integrity, and fully auditable throughout its lifecycle.

Supported evidence types

  • Photos and videos
  • Documents and PDFs
  • Audio recordings
  • Screenshots and digital artifacts
  • Interview transcripts

Evidence handling rules

  • All uploads are encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Evidence is tenant‑isolated and RBAC‑restricted
  • Metadata includes uploader, timestamp, and linked entity
  • Evidence cannot be deleted without administrative approval

Operational integration

Evidence is linked to incidents, cases, and investigations, and appears in context panels and audit logs.

Audit integrity

Every evidence action—including upload, view, and redaction—is logged with full detail for defensibility.
