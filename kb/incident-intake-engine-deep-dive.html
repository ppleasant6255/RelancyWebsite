Engine Overview

The Incident Intake Engine handles all incoming incident reports across web, mobile, kiosk, and hotline channels. Intake is validated, normalized, and routed using deterministic rules.

Intake stages

  • Submission capture
  • Field validation and normalization
  • Duplicate detection
  • Category and severity pre‑classification
  • Routing to triage or investigator

Operational safeguards

Intake cannot be deleted or altered without justification. Anonymous submissions follow enhanced privacy rules.

Audit integrity

Every intake event is logged with timestamp, channel, actor (or anonymous), and validation results.
