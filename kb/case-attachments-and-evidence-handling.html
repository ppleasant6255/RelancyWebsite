Case Attachments and Evidence Handling

How Relancy manages documents, media, and evidence associated with cases and investigations.

Back to Knowledge Base

Overview

Relancy provides secure, structured handling of attachments and evidence to support investigations, HR cases, and operational reviews. All attachments are stored in tenant-isolated storage with full audit logging.

Supported attachment types

Relancy supports common evidence formats, including:

  • Documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
  • Images (PNG, JPG)
  • Audio and video files
  • Exported logs or reports

Evidence integrity

Evidence is stored with metadata such as:

  • Upload timestamp
  • Uploader identity
  • Associated case or incident
  • File hash for integrity verification

Access control

Access to attachments is governed by role-based permissions. Sensitive evidence is restricted to authorized users only.

Audit logging

All attachment uploads, downloads, and deletions are logged in the audit trail.

Deletion rules

Evidence can be deleted only by authorized users and only when allowed by policy. Deletions are logged and do not remove historical audit entries.
