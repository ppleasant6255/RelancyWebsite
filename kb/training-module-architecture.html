Architecture Overview

Training modules are composed of structured content blocks, interactive assessments, and completion tracking. Modules support multimedia, branching logic, and compliance alignment.

Module components

  • Content sections (text, video, audio)
  • Knowledge checks and quizzes
  • Completion tracking
  • Accessibility controls
  • Versioning and update history

Operational safeguards

Training content cannot be altered without versioning. Assessments require minimum passing scores for compliance.

Audit integrity

Module views, completions, and assessment results are logged for compliance and reporting.
