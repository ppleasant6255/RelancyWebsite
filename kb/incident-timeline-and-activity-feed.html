Timeline Overview

The incident timeline provides a complete record of all actions taken on an incident, from submission through routing, classification, and linkage to cases or investigations.

Timeline components

  • Incident submission
  • Reporter updates
  • Classification and severity changes
  • Routing and escalation events
  • Evidence uploads
  • Linking to cases or investigations

Audit integrity

All timeline entries are logged with timestamp, actor, and triggering conditions for defensibility.
