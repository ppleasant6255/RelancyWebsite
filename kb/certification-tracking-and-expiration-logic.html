Certification Overview

Relancy tracks employee certifications, renewal cycles, and expiration dates. Certifications contribute to readiness scores and may be required for specific roles or locations.

Expiration logic

  • Automatic reminders before expiration
  • Readiness score reduction after expiration
  • Optional suspension of role eligibility
  • Manager and compliance notifications

Renewal workflows

Employees may upload renewal documentation or complete required training. Renewals update readiness scores automatically.

Audit integrity

All certification events—including issuance, renewal, and expiration—are logged for compliance and historical accuracy.
