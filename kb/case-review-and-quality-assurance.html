Case Review and Quality Assurance

How Relancy supports structured review of case quality, completeness, and compliance.

Overview

Case review ensures that cases are handled consistently, documented thoroughly, and compliant with policy. Relancy provides tools to support quality assurance across HR and Security teams.

Review criteria

  • Accurate classification
  • Complete lifecycle progression
  • Clear and neutral documentation
  • Evidence integrity
  • Corrective action follow‑through

Reviewer roles

Senior HR, Security, or compliance staff may perform case reviews depending on severity or policy.

Audit integration

Review outcomes are logged and may be included in compliance reports or leadership dashboards.

Continuous improvement

Review findings may inform training, policy updates, or workflow adjustments.
