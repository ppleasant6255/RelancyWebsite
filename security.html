How Guards Use It

Guards capture incidents quickly without changing how the floor actually works.

  • Select incident type (theft, disturbance, injury, trespass, etc.)
  • Confirm location and time
  • Enter a clear narrative in their own words
  • Attach photos, video stills, or documents
  • Submit to the Security domain only
Supervisor Workflow
Supervisors control quality, escalation, and follow‑through — without leaving Security.

Review, Correct, Approve

Supervisors see every incident in their queue and can act immediately.

  • Review guard narratives and attachments
  • Correct details or add missing context
  • Return to guard for clarification
  • Approve and lock the incident

Escalation Decisions

When an incident crosses a threshold, supervisors can escalate to Directors with one intentional action.

  • Termination or prosecution likely
  • Serious safety or criminal elements
  • Repeat offenders or pattern formation

AI Redacted Scanning
A safety net that never exposes content and never overrides human judgment.

Redacted First, Always

AI only sees a redacted version of the incident — names, identifiers, and protected classes are masked.

  • Detects threats, violence, weapons, intoxication
  • Flags repeat offenders and repeat locations
  • Surfaces timeline anomalies and contradictions
  • Never auto‑escalates or shares across domains

Flags, Not Decisions

AI can only flag incidents for review inside Security. Supervisors and Directors make the decisions.

  • No auto‑closing, no auto‑discipline
  • No cross‑domain visibility
  • No summaries sent to Directors
  • Human judgment remains in control
