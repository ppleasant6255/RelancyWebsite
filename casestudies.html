Operational Suite — Security-Led Organizations

Multi‑Site Retailer Reduces Incident Chaos

A regional retailer with 14 stores struggled with inconsistent incident reporting. Guards wrote free‑text narratives, supervisors corrected them manually, and Directors only saw incidents when something went wrong.

How Relancy Can Help

  • Structured incident capture for guards across all locations
  • Supervisor review workflow before escalation
  • Directors receive only intentional, clean escalations
  • Parking & TruckGate reduces gate delays
  • AI redaction ensures no PII or protected‑class information leaks

Expected Outcome: Improved incident quality and a significant reduction in surprise escalations for Directors.

Distribution Center Eliminates Supervisor Bottlenecks

A 24/7 logistics hub had supervisors drowning in incident reports. Many were incomplete, missing context, or escalated too early.

How Relancy Can Help

  • Structured supervisor review with clear required fields

  • Directors see only incidents that meet escalation criteria
  • TruckGate captures bill‑of‑lading issues consistently
  • AI redaction protects identities before analysis

Investigations Suite — HR‑Led or Mixed Governance

Healthcare System Strengthens Protected‑Class Integrity

A hospital network faced rising HR complaints involving protected classes and needed a system that enforced privacy and prevented investigators from seeing unnecessary identity details.

How Relancy Can Help

  • Pseudonymized narratives (Witness #1, Subject #2, etc.)
  • Automatic removal of protected‑class descriptors
  • Separated HR and Security domains with strict privacy
  • Cross‑domain linking allows safe pattern detection
  • Directors see full identities only after escalation

Expected Outcome: Significant improvement in defensibility during internal reviews.
