Pricing Philosophy

Relancy is not a per‑seat SaaS tool. It is an enterprise investigative platform designed to eliminate friction, reduce operational chaos, and protect organizations legally and politically.

  • Unlimited users — Security and HR should never ration access.
  • Unlimited incidents and cases — Investigations should never be throttled.
  • Governance‑first design — Privacy, redaction, and auditability are core, not add‑ons.

Most importantly, we will put together the most affordable package possible for each tenant, providing only the apps and features they actually need.
